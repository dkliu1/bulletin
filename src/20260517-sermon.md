# 使徒行传 1:6-26 及 灵恩节讲章

他们聚集的时候，问耶稣说：“主啊，你复兴以色列国就在这时候吗？”
Then they gathered around him and asked him, “Lord, are you at this time going to restore the kingdom to Israel?”

耶稣对他们说：“父凭着自己的权柄所定的时候、日期，不是你们可以知道的。
He said to them: “It is not for you to know the times or dates the Father has set by his own authority.

但圣灵降临在你们身上，你们就必得着能力，并要在耶路撒冷、犹太全地，和撒马利亚，直到地极，作我的见证。”
But you will receive power when the Holy Spirit comes on you; and you will be my witnesses in Jerusalem, and in all Judea and Samaria, and to the ends of the earth.”

说了这话，他们正看的时候，他就被取上升，有一朵云彩把他接去，便看不见他了。
After he said this, he was taken up before their very eyes, and a cloud hid him from their sight.

当他往上去，他们定睛望天的时候，忽然有两个人身穿白衣，站在旁边，说：
They were looking intently up into the sky as he was going, when suddenly two men dressed in white stood beside them.

“加利利人哪，你们为什么站着望天呢？这离开你们被接升天的耶稣，你们见他怎样往天上去，他还要怎样来。”
“Men of Galilee,” they said, “why do you stand here looking into the sky? This same Jesus, who has been taken from you into heaven, will come back in the same way you have seen him go into heaven.”

有一座山，名叫橄榄山，离耶路撒冷不远，约有安息日可走的路程。当下，门徒从那里回耶路撒冷去。
Then the apostles returned to Jerusalem from the hill called the Mount of Olives, a Sabbath day’s walk from the city.

到了，就上了所住的一间楼房；在那里有彼得、约翰、雅各、安得烈、腓力、多马、巴多罗买、马太、亚勒腓的儿子雅各、奋锐党的西门，还有雅各的儿子犹大。
When they arrived, they went upstairs to the room where they were staying. Those present were Peter, John, James and Andrew; Philip and Thomas, Bartholomew and Matthew; James son of Alphaeus and Simon the Zealot, and Judas son of James.

这些人同着几个妇人和耶稣的母亲马利亚，并耶稣的弟兄，都同心合意地恒切祷告。
They all joined together constantly in prayer, along with the women and Mary the mother of Jesus, and with his brothers.

那时，有许多人聚会，约有一百二十名，彼得站在弟兄中间，说：
In those days Peter stood up among the believers (a group numbering about a hundred and twenty)

“诸位弟兄！圣灵藉着大卫的口，在圣经上预言领人捉拿耶稣的犹大，这话是必须应验的。
and said, “Brothers and sisters, the Scripture had to be fulfilled in which the Holy Spirit spoke long ago through David concerning Judas, who served as guide for those who arrested Jesus.

他本来列在我们数中，并且在职任上得了一分。
He was one of our number and shared in our ministry.”

这人用他作恶的工价买了一块田，以后身子倒毙，肚腹崩裂，肠子都流出来。
(With the payment he received for his wickedness, Judas bought a field; there he fell headlong, his body burst open and all his intestines spilled out.

住在耶路撒冷的众人都知道这事，所以按着他们那里的话给那块田起名叫亚革大马，就是“血田”的意思。
Everyone in Jerusalem heard about this, so they called that field in their language Akeldama, that is, Field of Blood.)

因为诗篇上写着，说：愿他的住处变为荒场，无人在内居住；又说：愿别人得他的职分。
“For,” said Peter, “it is written in the Book of Psalms: “‘May his place be deserted; let there be no one to dwell in it,’ and, “‘May another take his place of leadership.’

所以，主耶稣在我们中间始终出入的时候，
Therefore it is necessary to choose one of the men who have been with us the whole time the Lord Jesus was living among us,

就是从约翰施洗起，直到主离开我们被接上升的日子为止，必须从那常与我们同在的人中立一位与我们同作耶稣复活的见证。”
beginning from John’s baptism to the time when Jesus was taken up from us. For one of these must become a witness with us of his resurrection.”

于是选举两个人，就是那称为巴撒巴，又名犹士都的约瑟，和马提亚。
So they nominated two men: Joseph called Barsabbas (also known as Justus) and Matthias.

众人就祷告说：“主啊，你知道万人的心，求你指明你所挑选的是哪一个，
Then they prayed, “Lord, you know everyone’s heart. Show us which of these two you have chosen

叫他得这使徒的职分。这位分犹大已经丢弃，往自己的地方去了。”
to take over this apostolic ministry, which Judas left to go where he belongs.”

于是众人掣签，掣出马提亚来；他就列在十一个使徒之中。
Then they cast lots, and the lot fell to Matthias; so he was added to the eleven apostles.

## 下周日是五旬节（圣灵降临节）。请翻找您的衣柜，打开抽屉，找一件红色的衣服，下周日穿着来教会。
Pentecost Sunday next week. Please dig up your closets, open your drawers and find something red to come to church next Sunday.

为什么呢？因为红色象征着同在、能力和已经实现的应许，证明我们的主是诚实的，非常诚实，他从不撒谎。
Why? Because red symbolizes the presence, the power and the promise which has come true proving our Lord is honest, very honest, he never lies.

所以，没有理由怀疑他，当我们觉得上帝不知道或不关心时，没有必要感到难过或忧愁。
So there is no point to doubt him, there is no need to feel bad or sad, when we think God doesn’t know or when we feel God doesn’t care.

约翰福音 14:16 耶稣说：“……我要求父，父就另外赐给你们一位保惠师，叫他永远与你们同在，就是真理的圣灵……”
John 14:16 Jesus said '... and I will ask the Father, and he will give you another Counselor to be with you forever - the Spirit of truth...

26 节：“……但保惠师，就是圣灵，他要将一切的事指教你们，并且要叫你们想起我对你们所说的一切话。”
26 the Counselor, the Holy Spirit will teach you all things and will remind you of everything I have said to you ....

约翰福音 15:26 “但我要从父那里差保惠师来，就是从父出来真理的圣灵；他来了，就要为我作见证。”
John 15:26 'When the Counselor comes, whom I will send to you from the Father, the Spirit of truth who goes out from the Father, he will testify about me.'

约翰福音 16:8 “他既来了，就要叫世人为罪、为义、为审判，自己责备自己。……他要引导你们明白一切的真理……他要将受于我的告诉你们，从而荣耀我。”
John 16:8 'When he comes, he will convict the world of guilt in regard to sin and righteousness and judgement... he will guide you into all truth... he will bring glory to me by taking from what is mine and making it known to you.'

这个应许，就像上帝所有其他的应许一样，已经实现并将继续实现。所以，请带着信心、喜乐和感恩信靠上帝。
This promise, like all other promises from God has been fulfilled and will be fulfilled. So trust God with confidence, with joy, with gratitude.

圣灵坚固并加添我们服侍的能力——无论是本地还是全球的服侍，是对边缘人还是上层阶级的服侍，从 West Footscray 到 Moorabbin 的服侍……
The Holy Spirit strengthens us and empowers our ministry, ministry local and global, ministry for the outcasts and the upper classes, ministry from West Footscray to Moorabbin ...

对黑人、白人、棕色人和黄种人的服侍。服侍不应只是单一文化的，也应该是多元文化的。耶稣在升天前这样说：
Ministry to the black, the white, the brown and the yellow, ministry should not be just mono, but also multicultural. Jesus said this before his ascension:

“但圣灵降临在你们身上，你们就必得着能力，并要在耶路撒冷、犹太全地，和撒马利亚，直到地极，作我的见证。”
“But you will receive power when the Holy Spirit comes on you; and you will be my witnesses in Jerusalem, and in all Judea and Samaria, and to the ends of the earth.”

基督的服侍是博大精深的，不应是浅薄和表面化的。我们应当祈祷并等待。等候上帝是灵命更新和预备的季节，绝不是浪费时间。
Christ’ ministry is broad and profound, it shouldn’t be shallow and superficial. We should pray and wait. To wait on God is a season of spiritual renewal and preparation, never a waste of time.

所以永远不要匆忙，不要急躁，当尚未看到完美时永远不要抱怨。
So never rush, never being impatient, and never complain when the perfection is not yet seen.

我们想知道什么时候会发生，为什么还没有发生，还要等多久？耶稣说：“父凭着自己的权柄所定的时候、日期，不是你们可以知道的。”
We want to know when will it happen, why it is not happening, how long more we have to wait? Jesus says ‘ it is not for you to know the times or dates the Father has set by his own authority.'

但圣灵降临在你们身上，你们就必得着能力……你们要受圣灵的洗……圣灵会在你们身上结出果子，使我们拥有仁爱、喜乐、和平、忍耐、恩慈、良善、信实、温柔和节制……
But you will receive power when the Holy Spirit comes on you … you will be baptised with the Holy Spirit.. the Holy Spirit will bear his fruit on you, so we will have love, joy, peace, patience , kindness, goodness, faithfulness , gentleness and self control…

圣灵曾帮助圣大卫堂选出了合适的领袖、合适的牧师 Kesha 博士，他在你们数月的祈祷后，在提名人勤勉忠心地搜寻、研究、访问、面试、分析和辨别之后即将到来……这一切都在圣灵的引导和指引下。
The Holy Spirit had helped St David’s to choose the right leader, the right vicar, the Rev Dr Kesha who is to come after your months of prayers, after the nominators’ diligent and faithful work to search and research, to visit and interview, to analyse and discern … all guided and directed by the Holy Spirit.

门徒在寻找新领袖接替加略人犹大时，也面临着同样的问题。当时只剩下 11 人。犹大在背叛耶稣后，因自己的罪恶感而选择自尽，他没有意识到自己可以祈求宽恕，无需用这种激烈而悲惨的方式处理自己的罪。
The disciples faced the same problem when they had to find a new leader to replace Judas Iscariot. There were only 11 left, Judas chose to kill himself because of his own sense of guilt after betraying Jesus, not realized he could ask for forgiveness but no need to deal with his sins with such a drastic and tragic way.

这 11 个人知道他们需要找人接替犹大，因为他们需要 12 位使徒来代表以色列的 12 个支派。
The 11 knew they needed to find someone to replace Judas, as they needed 12 apostles to represent the 12 tribes of Israel.

在马太福音 19:28 中，耶稣说：“我实在告诉你们：到复兴的时候，人子坐在他荣耀的宝座上，你们这些跟从我的人，也要坐在十二个宝座上，审判以色列十二个支派。”
In Matthew 19:28 Jesus said I tell you the truth, at the renewal of all things, when the Son of Man sits on his glorious throne you who have followed me will also sit on 12 thrones, judging the 12 tribes of Israel.

他们如何能轻易找到一位使徒的接替者呢？尽管犹大堕落并失去了资格，但他也是耶稣经过一夜或数夜的祷告后精心挑选的。
How could they easily find a substitute for an apostle? Though fallen and disqualified, Judas was selected carefully by Jesus over a night or nights of prayers.

这 11 个人也祷告了，但符合标准的人并不多。彼得自己作为使徒也曾有过惨痛的失败，但因着上帝的怜悯得到了赦免和恢复。
The 11 prayed as well, but not many fit the criteria. Peter who himself failed horribly as an apostle but was forgiven and reinstalled by God’s mercy.

彼得建议：“愿别人得他的职分。所以主耶稣在我们中间始终出入的时候，就是从约翰施洗起，直到主离开我们被接上升的日子为止，必须从那常与我们同在的人中立一位与我们同作耶稣复活的见证。”
Peter suggested: “'May another take his place of leadership.' Therefore it is necessary to choose one of the men who have been with us the whole time the Lord Jesus was living among us, beginning from John’s baptism to the time when Jesus was taken up from us. For one of these must become a witness with us of his resurrection.”

接替犹大的人必须是耶稣生平和事工的第一手见证人。圣经说他们提名了两个人：约瑟（又名犹士都）和马提亚。
The one who was to replace Judas must have a firsthand witness to the life and ministry of Jesus. The Bible says they had nominated two men: Joseph called Barsabbas (also known as Justus) and Matthias.

我们不确定我们的提名人有多少提名可供选择……我们不知道他们是谁。我们相信挑选牧师的过程有许多复杂而彻底的程序。
Well, we are not sure how many nominations our nominators had, and to choose from… we don’t know who they are. We believe there are many complex and thorough procedures for a vicar to be selected.

这肯定很困难，但也绝对容易，因为圣灵从头到尾都在引导和带领这个过程。因此，Kesha 博士最终被选中，我们都为此感到非常高兴。
It was surely hard but also definitely easy, for the Holy Spirit has been guiding and leading the process right from the very beginning to the very end. Thus Rev Dr Kesha was finally chosen and we are all very happy about it.

那么在五旬节之前，在圣灵降临之前，这 11 位使徒是如何决定谁是下一任继任者的呢？应该是约瑟还是马提亚，因为两个人都符合作为耶稣复活见证人的标准。
What about before Pentecost, before the descension of the Holy Spirit. How did the 11 apostles decide on who is the next successor? Should that be Joseph or should that be Matthias as both fit the criteria being a witness of Jesus’ resurrection.

这 11 个人祷告说：“主啊，你知道万人的心，求你指明你所挑选的是哪一个，叫他得这使徒的职分。这位分犹大已经丢弃，往自己的地方去了。” 于是众人掣签，掣出马提亚来；他就列在十一个使徒之中。
The 11 prayed “Lord, you know everyone’s heart. Show us which of these two you have chosen to take over this apostolic ministry, which Judas left to go where he belongs.” Then they cast lots, and the lot fell to Matthias; so he was added to the eleven apostles.

圣经时代的掣签是一种利用石头、木棍或任何有标记的代币来做决定或确定上帝旨意的方法……类似于现在的抛硬币、抽签或掷骰子。
Casting lots in Bible time was a method of making decisions or determining God’s will by using stones or sticks or any marked tokens… similar to flipping a coin, or drawing straws or, throwing a dice nowadays.

这听起来像是随机的或不虔诚的，但在圣灵正式降临之前，这是一种寻求神圣指引的严肃且可接受的方式。
It sounds random or ungodly, but it was a serious and acceptable way to seek divine guidance before the official coming of the Holy Spirit.

在约书亚记 18:6 中，约书亚说：“你们将地分作七分，描绘好了，拿到我这里来。我要在耶和华我们上帝面前为你们掣签。”
In Joshua 18:6, Joshua says, “When you have written a description of the seven portions of land and brought it to me, I will cast lots for you here in the presence of the Lord our God.”

利未记 16:8 告诉我们，摩西时代的以色列祭司亚伦，按照主的指示，在处理赎罪祭时掣签。
Leviticus 16:8 tells us that Aaron, the priest for Israel during the time of Moses cast lots at the direction of the Lord, concerning how to handle sin offerings.

在圣灵降临之前，掣签不仅仅被视为巧合。相反，它是上帝向他的子民启示他旨意的一种方式。
Before the coming of the Spirit, the casting of lots was not seen merely as chance. Rather, it was a way for God to reveal his will to His people.

我们在使徒行传第 1 章读到人们最后一次这样做。在圣灵降临之后，就没有再关于这种掣签做法的记录了。
We read in Acts 1 the last time people doing this. There is no other record of such practice of casting lots… after the coming of the Holy Spirit.

在使徒行传第 6 章中，当他们挑选七位执事去照顾那些在每日供给中被忽视的希腊寡妇时，并没有掣签。
Not in Acts 6 when they chose the 7 deacons to look after those Greek widows who were being overlooked in the daily distribution of food.

五旬节之后，我们现在有上帝的圣灵来带领我们。我们不再需要掣签。相反，我们必须向他发出我们的祷告，将我们的忧虑卸给他，向他表达我们的赞美，并且我们必须信靠他，顺服他。
After the Pentecost, we have God’s Holy Spirit to lead us now. We no longer have to cast lots. Rather, we must cast our prayers to Him, we must cast our cares upon Him, we must cast our admiration towards Him, and we must trust Him and obey Him.

## 让我们在下周日更多地学习和崇拜，届时我们庆祝五旬节，我们祈求：圣灵，请降临。阿们。
Let us learn more and worship more next Sunday, when we celebrate Pentecost, and we ask Come, Holy Spirit, please come. Amen.
