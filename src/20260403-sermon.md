受难节讲道词 | Good Friday Sermon

Jesus was arrested, interrogated, sentenced and executed. This confirmed John’s proclamation when Jesus was born “He was in the world… yet the world did not know him. He came to what was his own, and his own people did not accept him." (John 1:10-11) People cried "crucify him", "crucify him". Nowadays people say there is no God, there is no Jesus, there is no Good Friday, there is no Easter.

耶稣被捕、受审、判刑并被处决。这证实了约翰在耶稣降生时的宣告：“他在世界……世界却不认识他。他到自己的地方来，自己的人倒不接待他。”（约翰福音 1:10-11）当时的人们喊着：“钉他十字架！”“钉他十字架！”而现今的人们则说没有上帝，没有耶稣，没有受难节，也没有复活节。

We Christians believe Bible, we believe in church history. We are very sure that Jesus was stripped naked and nailed to a cross. There was no justice because an innocent man was killed. In fact, the Son of God was killed by the creatures of God who refused God’s rule and God’s salvation.

我们基督徒相信圣经，相信教会历史。我们深信耶稣曾被剥光衣服，钉在十字架上。那里没有正义，因为一个无辜的人被杀害了。事实上，上帝的儿子是被上帝所造的人杀害的，这些人拒绝了上帝的统治和救恩。

We believe Jesus, God in flesh, had to suffer and die so that we sinners are forgiven. There are no other ways of salvation but the Cross of salvation. The Cross is the only bridge that exists between us and a Holy God. It's the only way our sin can ever be forgiven.

我们相信耶稣——道成肉身的上帝——必须受苦并受死，好使我们罪人得赦免。除了拯救的十字架，没有别的救赎之路。十字架是连接我们与神圣上帝之间唯一的桥梁。这是我们的罪得蒙赦免的唯一途径。

We need to come to the Cross to understand the profound and sacrificial love of God. We need to come to the Cross to understand human sufferings, human agony, human troubles… all because of the human sins. Sent from heaven, saw the gravity of human sins, sorrowed to be hurt, hated, accused and excluded, Jesus chose not to abandon us and return to heaven, but to remain and be pierced at the Cross, so that He could be with us and care for us forever.

我们需要来到十字架前，去领悟上帝深沉且牺牲的爱。我们需要来到十字架前，去理解人类的苦难、痛苦和忧患……这一切皆因人的罪而起。耶稣从天而降，看到了人类罪孽的沉重；他因遭受伤害、憎恨、控告和排挤而忧伤，但他没有选择抛弃我们独自返回天堂，而是选择留下，在十字架上被刺透，好使他能永远与我们同在，永远看顾我们。

If Jesus had come only as God, then He would not have been able to die on the Cross for our sins because God cannot die. But man is mortal. So He came as Man and suffered as Man. The blood, sweat and tears were real. The pain was real. The death was real.

如果耶稣仅以上帝的身份降临，他便无法为我们的罪死在十字架上，因为上帝是不会死亡的。但人必有一死，所以他以人的身份降临，以人的身份受苦。那血、汗与泪水都是真实的。痛苦是真实的。死亡也是真实的。

Jesus could choose to go home to heaven and forget about the Cross and the painful death. If He refused the Cross or jumped down from the Cross, His Father would bring Him home, and the whole world would literally go to hell, because of sin.

耶稣本可以选择回到天堂，忘掉十字架和痛苦的死亡。如果他拒绝十字架，或从十字架上跳下来，他的父必会接他回家，而整个世界将因着罪，确确实实地走向地狱。

But Jesus loved us so much that He chose death and humiliation. He went all the way to the Cross, and he finished the daunting task on the Cross. Before the Cross, there were the stripes. The Bible says that “by His stripes we are healed”. (Isaiah 53:5) Jesus took every one of those painful lashes because He had to pay the full price for our wellbeing, not just physically, but mentally and spiritually.

但耶稣如此深爱我们，以至于他选择了死亡与羞辱。他义无反顾地走向十字架，并完成了十字架上那艰巨的使命。在十字架之前，是鞭伤。圣经说：“因他受的鞭伤，我们得医治。”（以赛亚书 53:5）耶稣承受了每一记痛苦的鞭打，因为他必须为我们的安好付出全部代价，这不仅是身体上的，更是心理和属灵上的。

The world is somber because their human crimes and human sins, are still unresolved and unforgiven. Christians are somber because of Christ’s profound love and human’s rejection of Christ. “Yet to all who received him, to those who believed in his name, he gave the right to become children of God” (John 1:12). We are so sad to see Jesus died, but we are so thankful for his achievement on the Cross, which brings us abundant and eternal life. Amen.

世界是忧郁的，因为人类的罪行与罪孽仍未解决，未获赦免。基督徒是忧郁的，因为感念基督深沉的爱，也因人类对基督的拒绝。“凡接待他的，就是信他名的人，他就赐他们权柄，作上帝的儿女。”（约翰福音 1:12）看到耶稣死去，我们深感悲恸；但对于他在十字架上所成就的功勋，我们满怀感恩，因为这带给我们丰盛且永恒的生命。阿们。
