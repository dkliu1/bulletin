# 甲年——2025年圣诞日

Year A - CHRISTMAS DAY 2025  
甲年——2025年圣诞日

Isaiah 52:7-10 Psalm 98  
以赛亚书 52:7-10；诗篇 98 篇

Hebrews 1:1-4  
希伯来书 1:1-4

John 1:1-14  
约翰福音 1:1-14

Loving Heavenly Father, as we celebrate the Incarnation of your Son, we pray for our dear sister Michelle, that you will bless, protect, strengthen, heal and restore her in body, mind and spirit that she may continue to serve you faithfully, this we pray in Jesus’ name. Amen.  
慈爱的天父，当我们庆祝你圣子的道成肉身之时，我们为亲爱的姐妹米歇尔祈祷，求你赐福、保护、坚固、医治并恢复她的身心灵，使她能继续忠心事奉你。我们奉耶稣的名祈求。阿们。

“And the Word became flesh and dwelt among us”.  
“道成了肉身，住在我们中间。”

This is how St John speaks of the Incarnation of God in Jesus.  
这是圣约翰描述上帝在耶稣里道成肉身的方式。

No mention of Mary, Joseph and baby in the manger/cowshed, shepherds and the other popular birth narrative images that everyone loves - but no one will tolerate for the birth of their own children.  
他并未提到马利亚、约瑟和躺在马槽或牛棚里的婴孩，也没有牧羊人和那些人人喜爱的诞生故事画面——但若是自己孩子的出生，恐怕没人能接受这样的场景。

St John the author of the fourth Gospel describes the Incarnation in abstract and philosophical terms.  
第四福音书的作者圣约翰，以抽象而哲学性的语言来描述道成肉身。

“In the beginning was the Word” Capital W (upper case) the Big W, not the supermarket.  
“太初有道”——这里的“道”是大写的 W，是伟大的“道”，而不是超市里的商品。

‘The Word was God’, what an odd way of speaking about God some may respond? What does it mean?  
“道就是神”，有人或许会觉得这种说法很奇怪：这是什么意思呢？

Words - there are all kinds of words, millions of words in all languages. Good words, bad words, deceptive words, scientific words, mathematical words, meaningless words, blasphemous words, and of course we all use text messages, WhatsApp is a very popular messenger service, I communicate with over 20 friends, relatives, on WhatsApp.  
词语——各种各样的词语，世界上有数以百万计的词汇。好词、坏词、欺骗性的词、科学用语、数学用语、毫无意义的词、亵渎的词。当然，我们也都使用短信和 WhatsApp 这样非常流行的通讯工具，我在 WhatsApp 上与二十多位朋友和亲人保持联系。

We hear, read, see, think and write millions of words every day.  
我们每天听到、读到、看到、思考并写下无数的词语。

You may remember the wonderful stage musical My Fair Lady.  
你或许记得那部精彩的音乐剧《窈窕淑女》。

Eliza Doolittle, played by Audrey Hepburn sang.  
由奥黛丽·赫本饰演的伊莉莎·杜立德唱道：

“Words, words, words, I’m so sick of words,”  
“词语、词语、词语，我已经厌倦了这些词语，”

Eliza was of the opinion that ‘talk is cheap’, so she insisted “Show me, show me now.”  
伊莉莎认为“空谈毫无价值”，于是她坚持说：“给我看，立刻让我看见。”

In the Incarnation, God did just that, “The Word became flesh, and dwelt among us, full of grace and truth”. (Full of: abounding in Grace and Truth).  
在道成肉身中，上帝正是这样做的：“道成了肉身，住在我们中间，充充满满地有恩典、有真理。”（“充满”意指丰丰富富、洋溢着恩典与真理。）

We live in a world that is full of lies and falsehoods, we are all liars from the top down; from the leaders of the nations.  
我们生活在一个充满谎言和虚假的世界里，从上到下——从各国领袖开始——我们都是说谎的人。

And grace, graciousness? I’m sure you would all agree grace and graciousness are seriously deficient in our world.  
至于恩典与温良，我相信大家都会同意：在这个世界里，它们是极其匮乏的。

THE WORD: - in New Testament Greek, the Word used by John is Logos from which are derived concepts like Logic and Reason.  
“道”：在新约希腊文中，约翰所用的“道”是 Logos，这个词衍生出“逻辑”“理性”等概念。

In Ancient Greek Philosophy it refers to the principle of reason that governs the universe.  
在古希腊哲学中，它指的是支配宇宙的理性原则。

The Logos was an impersonal force and it governed the laws of Reason, Nature, Science, Physics, mathematics, truth and philosophy.  
Logos 被视为一种非人格的力量，支配着理性、自然、科学、物理、数学、真理和哲学的法则。

It is wisdom - absolute and ultimate, the highest. The Governing Principle of the Universe, the Highest the Greek world could think of. It was an impersonal force.  
它被视为绝对而终极的智慧，是宇宙的最高统治原则，是希腊世界所能想到的最高概念，但它仍是非人格的力量。

The concept of the Word, also has Hebrew antecedents. The Gospel writer John was a Jew, and no doubt Hebrew thought also had an influence on him.  
“道”的观念同样有希伯来背景。福音书作者约翰是犹太人，毫无疑问，希伯来思想也深深影响了他。

‘Dabar’ in Hebrew referred to the “Word of God”.  
希伯来文中的 “Dabar” 指的是“神的话”。

For the Jew the word of God, was living, active, creative, powerful and life giving.  
对犹太人而言，神的话是活的、有行动力的、有创造力的、有能力并且赐生命的。

In the Creation narrative of Genesis 1, God spoke and the universe came into existence as a result.
