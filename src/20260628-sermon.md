# 马太福音 / Matthew 10:40-42

Matthew 10: 40 “Anyone who welcomes you welcomes me, and anyone who welcomes me welcomes the one who sent me. 41 Whoever welcomes a prophet as a prophet will receive a prophet’s reward, and whoever welcomes a righteous person as a righteous person will receive a righteous person’s reward. 42 And if anyone gives even a cup of cold water to one of these little ones who is my disciple, truly I tell you, that person will certainly not lose their reward.”  

马太福音 10:40 “人接待你们，就是接待我；接待我，就是接待那差我来的。41 人因为先知的名接待先知，必得先知所得的赏赐；人因为义人的名接待义人，必得义人所得的赏赐。42 无论何人，因为门徒的名，只把一杯凉水给这小子里的一个喝，我实在告诉你们，这人不能不得赏赐。”

May the words of my mouth and the meditation of my heart be pleasing in your sight, O Lord, my Rock and Redeemer. Amen.

耶和华我的磐石，我的救赎主啊，愿我口中的言语、心里的意念在你面前蒙悦纳。阿们。

Finally we are reading the final 3 verses of the 10th chapter of Matthew’s Gospel.

终于，我们读到了马太福音第十章的最后三节经文。

Finally I am delivering my final sermon here at St David’s Moorabbin.. after my …about 40 sermons I preached in the past 11 months since August 2025.

终于，我也在这里——莫拉宾圣大卫堂（St David’s Moorabbin）——发表我的最后一次讲道。自2025年8月以来的过去11个月里，我大约在这里宣讲了40篇道。

This is the lectionary reading, the lectionary choice, this is the divine choice of message, not mine, but it is a relevant message at a relevant timing that you as a congregation are getting so busy to attend to the matters of the coming and going of vicars, vicars as God’s messengers, vicars as God’s servants, vicars as God’s prophets.

这是经课集的选读，是经课的选择。这是神所拣选的信息，而非我的选择。但这是一个在关键时刻非常应景的信息——因为你们作为会众，正忙于应对牧师去留与更迭的事务。而牧师，正是神的使者、神的仆人、神的先知。

Matthew 10:40-42 emphasizes that how we treat God's messengers directly reflects our relationship with God. Jesus teaches that supporting His followers—even with something as simple as a cup of water—is considered an act of love toward Christ Himself, and promises that no act of such kindness goes unrewarded.

## 马太福音 10:40-42 强调，我们如何对待神的使者，直接反映了我们与神的关系。耶稣教导我们，支持祂的追随者——哪怕只是递上一杯凉水这样简单的事——都被视为对基督本人的爱，并应许这样的善行绝不会得不到赏赐。

In the Gospel reading today, Matthew 10:40 - Jesus tells His disciples, "Whoever receives you receives me, and whoever receives me receives the one who sent me." When people welcome, listen to, and support Christ's messengers, they are actually welcoming Jesus, God the Son, and God the Father is also welcome and honored.

## 在今天的福音书读经中，马太福音 10:40 —— 耶稣对祂的门徒说：“人接待你们，就是接待我；接待我，就是接待那差我来的。”当人们欢迎、倾听并支持基督的使者时，他们实际上是在欢迎耶稣（圣子上帝），而圣父上帝也同样受到欢迎与尊崇。

Jesus says this as well 10:41 "Whoever receives a prophet because he is a prophet will receive a prophet's reward..." God values those who honor and support His devoted servants. By supporting a person who serves God, you are supporting God’s work and you are receiving the same blessings and the same rewards as you are a co-worker of Christ, and a co-worker of those who serve Christ.

耶稣在 10:41 也这样说：“人因为先知的名接待先知，必得先知所得的赏赐……”神看重那些尊崇并支持祂忠心仆人的人。通过支持一个侍奉神的人，你就是在支持神的工作，你也将获得相同的福分与赏赐，因为你是基督的同工，也是那些侍奉基督之人的同工。

In verse 42, Jesus concludes: "And whoever gives one of these little ones even a cup of cold water because he is a disciple... will certainly not lose his reward." The "little ones" refers to the disciples, the messengers, the prophets, no matter how you think they are little, inexperienced, insignificant, however they are imperfect, not worthy as their work is not up to your standard… you still should serve them with a cup of cold water… at least… give them some basic respect and a benefit of the doubt….give them moral support and provide them with necessary practical provision… a cup of cold water, a pot of hot soup or casserole, a little present and a word of encouragement ….above all… make sure there is enough funds to pay their living allowance, their stipend, yes stipend … not salary … as you are not paying your servants, you are paying God’s servants on God’s behalf.

## 在第42节中，耶稣总结道：“无论何人，因为门徒的名，只把一杯凉水给这小子里的一个喝……这人不能不得赏赐。”这里的“小子”指的是门徒、使者和先知。无论你认为他们多么微小、缺乏经验或微不足道，无论他们如何不完美，或者因为工作达不到你的标准而显得不够称职……你仍然应该用一杯凉水来服侍他们……至少……给予他们基本的尊重和信任……给予他们精神上的支持，并提供必要的实际供应……一杯凉水、一锅热汤或砂锅菜、一份小礼物和一句鼓励的话……最重要的是……确保有足够的资金来支付他们的生活津贴，即他们的圣俸（Stipend）——是的，是圣俸，而不是薪水（Salary）——因为你们不是在给自己的雇员付薪水，而是代表神在供养神的仆人。

You know, being a prophet, being a representative for Christ is never easy. They will not be welcomed everywhere (10:14-15), and they can expect to experience the same hostility as Jesus often does, for they are “like sheep into the midst of wolves” (10:16). They can expect to encounter persecution and trials (10:17-23). They are under the spot light, critiques, scrutiny all the time. They need also be prepared for painful division within families, the tension, the toll, the stress, the burdens, the misunderstanding…. all because a faithful Christian minister or a worker, is expected to put Jesus’ mission above family loyalties (10:34-38)……

## 要知道，作先知、作基督的代表从来都不是一件容易的事。他们不会在到处都受欢迎（10:14-15），他们注定要经历像耶稣常常遭遇的那种敌意，因为他们“如同羊进入狼群”（10:16）。他们要准备好面对迫害和审判（10:17-23）。他们时刻生活在聚光灯下，面对着批评与审视。他们还需要为家庭中痛苦的分裂、紧张的关系、代价、压力、重担和误解做好准备……这一切都是因为，一个忠心的基督徒牧者或同工，被要求将耶稣的使命置于家庭忠诚之上（10:34-38）……

So Please, please apply your sympathy, empathy, understanding and consideration for those who take up a public ministry. For all of this risk and suffering, Jesus promises, “those who lose their life for my sake will find it” (10:39). “Whoever welcomes Jesus’ followers welcomes Jesus, and whoever welcomes Jesus also welcomes the Father who sent the Son” (10:40).

## 所以请，请务必对那些承担公开事工的人给予你们的同情、共情、理解和体谅。针对所有这些风险和苦难，耶稣应许说：“为我失丧生命的，将要得着生命”（10:39）。“接待耶稣追随者的，就是接待耶稣；接待耶稣的，也就是接待那差圣子来的圣父”（10:40）。

If Jesus himself welcome, appreciate, honor those who serve him, even those who serve him with failures and mistakes, how much more we do the same to welcome, to appreciate, to honor …to be patient and forgiving… walk an extra mile, do an extra favour to those who are called to serve at St David’s.

## 如果耶稣自己都欢迎、赏识并尊崇那些服侍祂的人——甚至是那些在服侍中带有失败和错误的人，我们岂不更应该同样去欢迎、赏识和尊崇……去保持耐心与宽容……为那些受召在圣大卫堂服侍的人多走一里路、多给予一份恩惠吗？

Once again : “Whoever welcomes a prophet in the name of a prophet will receive a prophet’s reward; and whoever welcomes a righteous person in the name of a righteous person will receive the reward of the righteous” (10:41).

再次重申：“人因为先知的名接待先知，必得先知所得的赏赐；人因为义人的名接待义人，必得义人所得的赏赐”（10:41）。

For those who pay the costly price to support the ongoing Christian ministry Jesus says, “Rejoice and be glad, for your reward is great in heaven” (5:12). The righteous ones, you, you all who are sitting here, or online listening, you are promised that you “will shine like the sun in the kingdom of their Father” (13:43).

对于那些付出高昂代价来支持持续不懈的基督徒事工的人，耶稣说：“应当欢喜快乐，因为你们在天上的赏赐是大的”（5:12）。你们这些义人——所有坐在这里或在网上聆听的人——都得到了应许，你们“在他们父的国里，要发出光来，像太阳一样”（13:43）。

Jesus says : John 12: 26 Whoever serves me must follow me; and where I am, my servant also will be. My Father will honor the one who serves me.

耶稣说：约翰福音 12:26 “若有人服侍我，就当跟从我；我在哪里，服侍我的人也要在哪里。若有人服侍我，我父必尊重他。”

Jehovah Jireh , The Lord will provide. ( Genesis 22: 14 ) The Lord provided a ram to be sacrificed, so that Isaac was released and freed to fulfil God’s plan. We all could be a ram, serve as a provision for those who are charged to take up a daunting ministry. We all could be a raven, who brought bread and meat to prophet Elijah when he was hungry, when he was in needs.

耶和华以勒，耶和华必预备（创世记 22:14）。耶和华预备了一只公羊作燔祭，使以撒得以释放并自由地去成就神的计划。我们都可以成为那只公羊，为那些受命承担艰巨事工的人提供供应。我们也都可以成为那只乌鸦，在先知以利亚饥饿、有需要的时候，为他叼来饼和肉。

To conclude, my final word is to thank you thank you and thank you. You have been most loving, honor, showing reverence to me and to my ministry. You provided me, and paid me, very well. I enjoyed everyone of you, and cherished everyday of my ministry here. You have enriched my life and you have given me so much encouragement and confidence so that I may go further and further in my ministry.

## 最后，我的结束语是感谢你们，感谢你们，再感谢你们。你们对我以及我的事工展现了极大的爱心、尊重与敬意。你们供养我，给了我很好的保障。我喜爱你们每一个人，并珍惜我在这里服侍的每一天。你们丰富了我的生命，给了我如此多的鼓励与信心，使我能够在我的事工道路上走得更远。

I know, and I pray that you will do very well, extraordinary well for Dr Kezha, for Rev Paul Samuel, and for all who work hard among you, who preach and teach, who direct the affairs of the church, they are indeed worthy of the double honor, they are indeed worthy of your all around support.

我知道，并且我祈祷，你们会为 Dr Kezha、为 Rev Paul Samuel 以及所有在你们当中劳苦、传道教导、治理教会事务的人做到最好、做得格外好。他们理应受到加倍的敬奉，理应得到你们全方位的支持。

Cheers, Alleluia, Goodbye ! I praise the Lord, for St David’s past, for St David’s present, for St David’s better future ….. in the name of the Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit . Amen

祝愿大家，哈利路亚，再见！我赞美主，因着圣大卫堂的过去、圣大卫堂的现在，以及圣大卫堂更美好的未来……奉圣父、圣子、圣灵的名。阿们。
