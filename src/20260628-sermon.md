## 主日讲道:接待神的仆人 (Sermon: Welcoming God'sServants)

## 马太福音 10:40-42 (Matthew 10:40-42)
「人接待你们就是接待我;接待我就是接待那差我来的。人因为先知的名接待先知,必得先知所得的赏赐;人
因为义人的名接待义人,必得义人所得的赏赐。无论何人,因为门徒的名,只把一杯凉水给这小子里的一个
喝,我实在告诉你们,这人不能不得赏赐。」
"Anyone who welcomes you welcomes me, and anyone who welcomes me welcomes the one who sent me. Whoever
welcomes a prophet as a prophet will receive a prophet’s reward, and whoever welcomes a righteous person as a
righteous person will receive a righteous person’s reward. And if anyone gives even a cup of cold water to one of these
little ones who is my disciple, truly I tell you, that person will certainly not lose their reward."

我口中的言语,心里的意念,愿在你面前蒙悦纳。耶和华——我的磐石,我的救赎主啊!阿们。
May the words of my mouth and the meditation of my heart be pleasing in your sight, O Lord, my Rock and Redeemer.
Amen.
## 最后,我们正在读马太福音第十章的最后三节经文。
Finally we are reading the final 3 verses of the 10th chapter of Matthew’s Gospel.
最后,这也是我在莫拉宾圣大卫堂(St David’s Moorabbin)做的最后一次讲道......自2025年8月以来的过去11个
月里,我在这里大约讲了40篇道。
Finally I am delivering my final sermon here at St David’s Moorabbin.. after my ...about 40 sermons I preached in the past 11
months since August 2025.
这是三代教会年历(Lectionary)的经文,是年历的挑选,这是神圣信息的主权选择,而不是我的选择。但在
当下这个特别的时刻,这是一条极其相关的信息——因为你们作为会众,正忙于处理牧师们(Vicars)的来临
与离去。牧师是神的使者,牧师是神的仆人,牧师是神的先知。
This is the lectionary reading, the lectionary choice, this is the divine choice of message, not mine, but it is a relevant
message at a relevant timing that you as a congregation are getting so busy to attend to the matters of the coming and
going of vicars, vicars as God’s messengers, vicars as God’s servants, vicars as God’s prophets.
马太福音 10:40-42 强调,我们如何对待神的使者,直接反映了我们与神的关系。耶稣教导我们,支持祂的追随
者——即使是像一杯凉水这样简单的事情——也被视为对基督本身的爱,并应许这样的恩慈之举绝不会得不到
赏赐。
Matthew 10:40-42 emphasizes that how we treat God's messengers directly reflects our relationship with God. Jesus
teaches that supporting His followers—even with something as simple as a cup of water—is considered an act of love
toward Christ Himself, and promises that no act of such kindness goes unrewarded
1

在今天的福音书读经中,马太福音 10:40,耶稣告诉祂的门徒:“谁接待你们,就是接待我;谁接待我,就是接
待那差我来的。”当人们欢迎、聆听并支持基督的使者时,他们实际上是在接待耶稣(圣子上帝),而圣父上帝
也同样受到接待与尊崇。
In the Gospel reading today, Matthew 10:40, Jesus tells His disciples, "Whoever receives you receives me, and whoever
receives me receives the one who sent me." When people welcome, listen to, and support Christ's messengers, they are actually
welcoming Jesus, God the Son, and God the Father is also welcome and honored.
耶稣在 10:41 也这样说:“人因为先知的名接待先知,必得先知所得的赏赐......”神看重那些尊崇并支持祂挚爱
仆人的人。通过支持一个服侍神的人,你就是在支持神的工作,你将获得与基督的同工以及与服侍基督之人相
同的祝福和赏赐。
Jesus says this as well 10:41 "Whoever receives a prophet because he is a prophet will receive a prophet's reward..." God
values those who honor and support His devoted servants. By supporting a person who serves God, you are supporting God’s
work and you are receiving the same blessings and the same rewards as you are a co-worker of Christ, and a co-worker of
those who serve Christ.
在第42节,耶稣总结道:“无论何人,因为门徒的名,只把一杯凉水给这小子里的一个喝......这人不能不得赏
赐。”这些“小子”指的是门徒、使者、先知,无论你认为他们多么微小、缺乏经验或微不足道,无论他们多么不
完美、甚至因为工作没有达到你的标准而显得不配......你仍然应该至少用一杯凉水来服侍他们......给他们一些
基本的尊重和包容......给他们精神上的支持,并为他们提供必要的物质供应——一杯凉水、一锅热汤或炖菜、
一件小礼物和一句鼓励的话......最重要的是,确保有足够的资金来支付他们的生活津贴和薪俸(stipend)——
是的,是薪俸,而不是普通的工资——因为你们不是在给自己的雇员付钱,而是代表神向神的仆人支付供应。
In verse 42, Jesus concludes: "And whoever gives one of these little ones even a cup of cold water because he is a
disciple... will certainly not lose his reward." The "little ones" refers to the disciples, the messengers, the prophets, no
matter how you think they are little, inexperienced, insignificant, however they are imperfect, not worthy as their work
is not up to your standard... you still should serve them with a cup of cold water... at least... give them some basic
respect and a benefit of the doubt....give them moral support and provide them with necessary practical provision... a
cup of cold water, a pot of hot soup or casserole, a little present and a word of encouragement ....above all... make sure
there is enough funds to pay their living allowance, their stipend, yes stipend ... not salary ... as you are not paying
your servants, you are paying God’s servants on God’s behalf.
要知道,作一个先知、作基督的代表从来都不是一件容易的事。他们不会在每个地方都受到欢迎
(10:14-15),他们可以预料到会经历和耶稣常常经历的相同的敌意,因为他们如同“羊进入狼群”(10:16)。
他们可以预料到会遭遇迫害和审判(10:17-23)。他们始终处于聚光灯下,面对着批评和审视。他们还需要为
家庭内部痛苦的分裂做好准备——那些紧张、折磨、压力、重担和误解......这一切都是因为,一个忠心的基督
徒牧者或工人,被要求将耶稣的使命置于家庭忠诚之上(10:34-38)......
You know, being a prophet, being a representative for Christ is never easy. They will not be welcomed everywhere (10:14-15),
and they can expect to experience the same hostility as Jesus often does, for they are “like sheep into the midst of wolves”
(10:16). They can expect to encounter persecution and trials (10:17-23). They are under the spot light, critiques, scrutiny all the
time. They need also be prepared for painful division within families, the tension, the toll, the stress, the burdens, the
misunderstanding.... all because a faithful Christian minister or a worker, is expected to put Jesus’ mission above family
loyalties (10:34-38)......

2

所以,请、请对那些承担公开事工的人给予你们的同情心、同理心、理解和体谅。针对所有这些风险和痛苦,
耶稣应许说:“凡为我丧失生命的,将要得着生命”(10:39)。“凡接待耶稣门徒的,就是接待耶稣;凡接待耶
稣的,也就是接待那差遣圣子来的圣父”(10:40)。
So Please, please apply your sympathy, empathy, understanding and consideration for those who take up a public
ministry . For all of this risk and suffering, Jesus promises, “those who lose their life for my sake will find it” (10:39).
“Whoever welcomes Jesus’ followers welcomes Jesus, and whoever welcomes Jesus also welcomes the Father who sent
the Son” (10:40).
如果耶稣自己都欢迎、赞赏并尊崇那些服侍祂的人——甚至是那些在服侍中带有失败和错误的人,那么我们更
应当如何去欢迎、赞赏、尊崇......去保持耐心和宽容......为那些被召在圣大卫堂服侍的人多走一里路,多给予
一份恩待。
If Jesus himself welcome, appreciate, honor those who serve him, even those who serve him with failures and mistakes, how
much more we do the same to welcome, to appreciate, to honor ...to be patient and forgiving... walk an extra mile, do an extra
favour to those who are called to serve at St David’s.
再次强调:“人因为先知的名接待先知,必得先知所得的赏赐;人因为义人的名接待义人,必得义人所得的赏
赐”(10:41)。对于那些付出高昂代价来支持持续的基督徒事工的人,耶稣说:“应当欢喜快乐,因为你们在天
上的赏赐是大的”(5:12)。义人们——你们,所有坐在这里或在网络上聆听的人——你们得到应许:“在他们
父的国里,要发出光来,像太阳一样”(13:43)。
Once again: “Whoever welcomes a prophet in the name of a prophet will receive a prophet’s reward; and whoever welcomes a
righteous person in the name of a righteous person will receive the reward of the righteous” (10:41). For those who pay the
costly price to support the ongoing Christian ministry Jesus says, “Rejoice and be glad, for your reward is great in heaven”
(5:12). The righteous ones, you, you all who are sitting here, or online listening, you are promised that you “will shine like the
sun in the kingdom of their Father” (13:43).

约翰福音 12:26 (John 12:26)
「若有人服事我,就当跟从我;我在哪里,服事我的人也要在那里;若有人服事我,我父必尊重他。」
"Whoever serves me must follow me; and where I am, my servant also will be. My Father will honor the one who serves
me."

以勒——耶和华必预备(创世记 22:14)。耶和华预备了一只公羊作祭物,从而使以撒得以释放和自由,去成就
神的计划。我们都可以成为那只公羊,为那些受命承担艰巨事工的人提供供应。我们也都可以成为那只乌鸦,
在先知以利亚饥饿缺乏时,为他送去饼和肉。
Jehovah Jireh, The Lord will provide. (Genesis 22:14) The Lord provided a ram to be sacrificed, so that Isaac was released and
freed to fulfil God’s plan. We all could be a ram, serve as a provision for those who are charged to take up a daunting ministry.
We all could be a raven, who brought bread and meat to prophet Elijah when he was hungry, when he was in needs.

To conclude, my final word is to thank you thank you and thank you. You have been most loving, honor, showing

everyday of my ministry here. You have enriched my life and you have given me so much encouragement and

I know, and I pray that you will do very well, extraordinary well for Dr Kezha, for Rev Paul Samuel, and for all who work hard
