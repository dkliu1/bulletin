## Let Us Pray  
## 同心祷告

丰盛的上帝，求你赐给本堂一位忠心的牧者，  
能忠实传讲你的话语，施行你的圣礼；  
成为鼓励者，装备你的子民参与服事，  
使我们能完成你所托付的呼召。  

求你赐给负责遴选的人智慧、分辨力和忍耐；  
也赐给我们温暖慷慨的心。  
奉主耶稣基督的名祷告，阿们。

Bountiful God, give to this parish a faithful pastor who will faithfully speak your word and minister your sacraments;  
an encourager who will equip your people for ministry and enable us to fulfil our calling.  
Give to those who choose wisdom, discernment, and patience,  
and to us give warm and generous hearts, for Christ’s sake. Amen.
