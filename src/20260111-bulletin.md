## Let Us Pray  
**让我们祷告**

Bountiful God, give to this parish a faithful pastor who will faithfully speak your word and minister your sacraments; an encourager who will equip your people for ministry and enable us to fulfil our calling. Give to those who will choose, wisdom, discernment and patience, and to us give warm and generous hearts, for Christ’s sake. Amen  
慈爱的上帝，求你赐给本堂会一位忠心的牧者，忠实宣讲你的话语，施行你的圣礼；成为鼓励者，装备你的子民参与事奉，使我们能完成你所呼召的使命。也求你赐给负责遴选的人智慧、分辨力与耐心，并赐给我们温暖慷慨的心，为主基督的缘故。阿们。

## A Birthday Prayer  
**生日祷告**

May the peace of God be with you on your Birthday. May His gifts of grace surround you as you follow in his way. May His blessings rest upon you, and His wisdom guide you on your birth date and always in all places. May God the Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit protect you and provide for you everyday and in every way. Amen.  
愿上帝的平安在你生日之日与你同在；当你行在祂的道路上，愿祂恩典的礼物环绕你；愿祂的祝福临到你，祂的智慧引导你，在你生日之日并在任何时间、任何地方。愿圣父、圣子、圣灵天天、时时保护并供应你。阿们。
