# 创世记第18章与“笑”的属灵反思 / Genesis 18 & Spiritual Reflections on "Laughter"

18 耶和华在幔利橡树那里，向亚伯拉罕显现出来。那时正热，亚伯拉罕坐在帐棚门口，
The LORD appeared to Abraham near the great trees of Mamre while he was sitting at the entrance to his tent in the heat of the day.

2 举目观看，见有三个人在对面站着。他一见，就从帐棚门口跑去迎接他们，俯伏在地，
Abraham looked up and saw three men standing nearby. When he saw them, he hurried from the entrance of his tent to meet them and bowed low to the ground.

3 说：“我主，我若在你眼前蒙恩，求你不要擦过你仆人前往。
He said, “If I have found favor in your eyes, my lord, do not pass your servant by.

4 容我拿点水来，你们洗洗脚，在树下歇息歇息。
Let a little water be brought, and then you may all wash your feet and rest under this tree.

5 容我拿点饼来，你们可以加添心力，然后往前去。你们既到仆人这里来，理当如此。”他们说：“就依你的话行吧。”
Let me get you something to eat, so you can be refreshed and then go on your way—now that you have come to your servant.” “Very well,” they answered, “do as you say.”

6 亚伯拉罕急忙进帐棚见撒拉，说：“你速速拿三细亚细面调和作饼。”
So Abraham hurried into the tent to Sarah. “Quick,” he said, “get three seahs of the finest flour and knead it and bake some bread.”

7 亚伯拉罕又跑去牛群里，牵了一只又嫩又好的牛犊来，交给仆人，仆人急忙预备好了。
Then he ran to the herd and selected a choice, tender calf and gave it to a servant, who hurried to prepare it.

8 亚伯拉罕又取了奶油和奶，并预备好的牛犊来，摆在他们面前，自己在树下站在旁边，他们就吃了。
He then brought some curds and milk and the calf that had been prepared, and set these before them. While they ate, he stood near them under a tree.

9 他们问亚伯拉罕说：“你妻子撒拉在哪里？”他说：“在帐棚里。”
“Where is your wife Sarah?” they asked him. “There, in the tent照着他说。”

10 三人中有一位说：“到明年这时候，我必要回到你这里；你的妻子撒拉必生一个儿子。”撒拉在那人后头的帐棚门口也听见了。
Then one of them said, “I will surely return to you about this time next year, and Sarah your wife will have a son.” Now Sarah was listening at the entrance to the tent, which was behind him.

11 亚伯拉罕和撒拉年纪老迈，撒拉的月经已经停了。
Abraham and Sarah were already very old, and Sarah was past the age of childbearing.

12 撒拉心里暗笑，说：“我既衰败，我主也老迈，岂能有这喜事呢？”
So Sarah laughed to herself as she thought, “After I am worn out and my lord is old, will I now have this pleasure?”

13 耶和华对亚伯拉罕说：“撒拉为什么暗笑，说：‘我既年老，果真能生养吗？’
Then the LORD said to Abraham, “Why did Sarah laugh and say, ‘Will I really have a child, now that I am old?’

14 耶和华岂有难成的事吗？到了日期，明年这时候，我必回到你这里，撒拉必生一个儿子。”
Is anything too hard for the LORD? I will return to you at the appointed time next year, and Sarah will have a sononn.”

15 撒拉害怕，就否认说：“我没有笑。”那人说：“不然，你实在笑了。”
Sarah was afraid, so she lied and said, “I did not laugh.” But he said, “Yes, you did laugh.”

## 有人在说谎。有人在掩盖。有人在否认自己的错误，却不知上帝知道一切，祂看透我们的内心。祂知道我们何时诚实，何时虚假……祂精确地知道我们何时在欺骗，何时在伪装。
Someone is lying. Someone is covering up. Someone is denying his / her wrongs, not knowing God knows everything , He knows our heart, he knows when we are honest and when we are not…. he knows exacting when we are deceiving, when we are pretending.

在幔利的希伯仑，三位特殊的客人来拜访亚伯拉罕。亚伯拉罕立刻察觉到，这些人并非寻常的访客，而是尊贵的上主使者。因此，他极其虔敬和尊重地迎接他们，谦卑地俯伏在地，称呼他们为“我主”，恳求他们不要擦过他而走。他用最好的食物款待他们，这规格只适合贵宾（VIP），或者说是化身为人、极其尊贵的上主使者（VID）。
In Mamre Hebron, three special guests came to visit Abraham. Abraham sensed it right away, these were not ordinary visitors, but superior divine beings, so he greeted them with reverence and respect, he bowed to them in humility , he bowed low to the ground , he addressed them as my Lord, he begged them not to bypass him, he served them the best food which fit only the VIP, the very important people, or the VID the very important divinity, in human disguise.

## 神圣的使者此行是为了确认并重申上帝不久前在创世记第17章中所立的应许——即撒拉虽然年纪已经很大，却仍将为亚伯拉罕生一个儿子（17:17）。当时听到这个应许，亚伯拉罕并没有说“谢谢主”，而是俯伏在地笑了起来，心里说：“一百岁的人还能得孩子吗？撒拉九十岁还能生养吗？”
The divinity came to confirm and reassure God’s promise a short time ago in Genesis 17 that Sarah, though very old, will bear Abraham a son, 17:17, hearing that promise, instead of saying ‘ thank you Lord’, Abraham fell facedown , he laughed ha ha ha and said to himself, ‘ Will a son be born to a man a hundred years old? Will Sarah bear a child at age of ninety?

如今，化身为人的神圣使者再次向他们确认……18:10 “到明年这时候，我必要回到你这里；你的妻子撒拉必生一个儿子。” 撒拉在那人后头的帐棚门口也听见了。11 亚伯拉罕和撒拉年纪老迈，撒拉的月经已经停了。撒拉是如何回应的呢？12 撒拉心里也暗笑，说：“我既衰败，我主也老迈，岂能有这喜事呢？”
Now the divine being in human form came to reaffirm with them… 18: 10 “I will surely return to you about this time next year, and Sarah your wife will have a son.” Now Sarah was listening at the entrance to the tent, which was behind him. 11 Abraham and Sarah were already very old, and Sarah was past the age of childbearing. How did Sarah respond? 12 So Sarah also laughed ha ha ha, as she thought, “After I am worn out and my lord is old, will I now have this pleasure?”

亚伯拉罕对上帝的应许发笑，撒拉也是如此，她对上帝的应许感到好笑……13 耶和华对亚伯拉罕说：“撒拉为什么暗笑，说：‘我既年老，果真能生养吗？’……15 撒拉害怕（担心上帝因她的怀疑而不悦），就否认说：“我没有笑。”那人说：“不然，你实在笑了。”
Abraham laughed at God’s promise, Sarah too, she laughed at God’s promise… 13 Then the LORD said to Abraham, “Why did Sarah laugh and say, ‘Will I really have a child, now that I am old?’ … 15 Sarah was afraid( that God was not happy because she doubted ) , so she lied and said, “I did not laugh.”But God said, “Yes, you did laugh.”

在圣经中，“笑”和“喜笑”这两个词大约出现了40次，其含义因语境的不同而发生剧烈的转变。圣经中的笑可以归类为神圣的喜乐、属天的胜利，但也可以归类为狐疑的试探、不信或嘲弄的讥笑。
In the Bible, the terms "laugh" and "laughter" appear roughly 40 times, with meanings that radically shift depending on the context. Biblical laughter can be categorized into holy joy, divine triumph, but also incredulous disbelief or mocking ridicule.

对于亚伯拉罕和撒拉而言，他们对上帝的应许表现出了怀疑和不信。他们一边笑，一边质问上帝：怎么可能，像我们这样年纪老迈的人怎么可能还会生孩子呢？
For Abraham and Sarah, they demonstrated their sceptical disbelief to God's promises , they were laughing and questioning God, how, how will we have a child in such an old age.

## 上帝说：14 “耶和华岂有难成的事吗？到了日期，明年这时候，我必回到你这里，撒拉必生一个儿子。” 耶和华确实没有一件难成的事。对耶和华来说，知道我们的感受、我们是信还是不信，这并不难。对耶和华来说，知道撒拉在说谎、在否认自己笑过，这并不难。她之所以暗笑，完全是因为不信，直到创世记第21章以撒出生后……撒拉抱着她那奇迹般的婴孩，再次笑了起来，而这一次，完全是出于纯粹的惊奇与喜乐。亚伯拉罕给孩子起名叫以撒，意思是“喜笑”，以此永远提醒着这两个时刻：一是他们因怀疑和不信而发笑的时刻，二是他们因充满喜乐地领受上帝所应许的福分最终成就而发笑的时刻。创世记21:6 撒拉说：“上帝使我喜笑，凡听见的必与我一同喜笑。”
God said 14 Is anything too hard for the LORD? I will return to you at the appointed time next year, and Sarah will have a son.” Indeed there isn’t anything too hard for the Lord. It is not hard for the Lord to know how we feel, whether we believe , whether we don’t believe . It is not hard for the Lord to know Sarah was lying, she was denying that she laughed , because she didn’t believe she would have a son, Sarah laughed to herself out of sheer disbelief, until in Gen 21 after Isaac was born… Sarah was holding her miracle baby, she laughed again this time truly in pure amazement and joy.. Abraham named the baby Isaac, which means laughter, serving as a permanent reminder of both moments, when they laughed to doubt and didn’t believe, then they laughed to joyfully receive the blessing which God has promised really came true. Genesis 21: 6 Sarah said, ‘ God has brought me laughter , and everyone who hears about this will laugh with me. ‘

Laughs and laughter to thank God, to count the blessings, to be optimistic, to be positive, to demonstrate faith, to look back and to recall so many times, so so many times that God has helped us, cured us, protected us, guided us .. the conception, the child births, the dream car, the dream house, the dream holiday, the tours , the cruises… when we agree and believe in God’s goodness, should we laugh, should we smile….Proverbs 17:22 a cheerful heart is a good medicine… so we should laugh more, we should have some laughter therapy, go and see a comedy, give yourself a good laugh, relax , be joyful, be happy…

不要再对上帝抱有太多的挑剔、苛刻、愤世嫉俗或怀疑。路加福音第8章中，睚鲁的女儿死了，所有人都在为她哀哭捶胸。耶稣说：“不要哭，她不是死了，是睡着了。” 第53节：他们晓得女儿已经死了，就嗤笑耶稣。但耶稣拉着跨她的手，呼叫说：“女儿，起来吧！”她的灵魂便回来，她立刻起来了……我们相信吗？我们应当相信吗？我们相信，所以我们喜笑，我们非常快乐。而对于那些不信的人，他们的笑则是嘲弄、讥讽和不敬。
Stop being too critical, difficult , cynical or sceptical towards God.
