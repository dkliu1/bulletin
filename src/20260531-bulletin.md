## A Prayer on Trinity Sunday
## 三一主日祷文

Almighty and everlasting God, you have given to us your servants grace by the confession of a true faith to acknowledge the glory of the eternal Trinity, and in the power of the divine majesty to worship the Unity: keep us steadfast in this faith, and evermore defend us from all adversities, for you live and reign, one God, for ever and ever. Amen.

全能永恒的上帝，祢赐恩给我们，使我们因真实信仰的宣认而认识永恒三一的荣耀，并在神圣威严的大能中敬拜独一真神。求祢坚固我们的信心，并永远保护我们脱离一切患难，因为祢是永活掌权、直到永永远远的独一真神。阿们。

## A Prayer on Reconciliation Sunday
## 和好主日祷文

Lord God, bring us together as one, reconciled with You and reconciled with each other. You made us in Your likeness, and You gave us Your Son Jesus Christ. He has given us forgiveness from sin. Lord God, bring us together as one, different in culture, but given new life in Jesus Christ, together as Your body, Your Church, Your people. Lord God, bring us together as one, reconciled, healed, forgiven, sharing You with others as You have called us to do. In Jesus Christ, let us be together as one. Amen.

主上帝，求祢使我们合而为一，与祢和好，也彼此和好。祢按自己的形象创造我们，并赐下祢的儿子耶稣基督，使我们得着罪的赦免。主啊，求祢使我们虽有不同文化，却在基督里得着新生命，同作祢的身体、祢的教会、祢的子民。求祢使我们成为一个蒙和好、得医治、被赦免的群体，并照祢的呼召将祢分享给他人。在耶稣基督里，愿我们同心合一。阿们。
