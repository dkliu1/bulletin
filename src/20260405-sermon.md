# Year A - EASTER DAY 5/4/26
# 甲年 - 复活日 2026年4月5日

Acts 10:34-43 | Psalm 118:1-24
使徒行传 10:34-43 | 诗篇 118:1-24

Colossians 3:1-4 | Matthew 28:1-10
歌罗西书 3:1-4 | 马太福音 28:1-10

Recently we were honoured by a visit from our children and grandchildren. We went out to our back garden, where we have a Judas Tree (Cercis Siliquastrum). Incidentally according to tradition, this was the tree that Judas hanged himself on, therefore Judas Tree.
最近，我们很荣幸地迎来了孩子和孙辈们的探访。我们一起去了后花园，那里种着一棵犹大树（西洋紫荆）。顺便提一下，根据传统，这就是犹大自缢的那种树，因此得名犹大树。

Our granddaughters picked up a couple of seed pods and opened them and got a few seeds out, then planted them in a little pot and are eagerly waiting to see new Judas Tree plants grow from them. They knew new life would sprout from them.
我的小孙女们捡起几个种荚并剥开，取出几粒种子，种在一个小花盆里，正急切地等待着新的犹大树苗破土而出。她们知道，新的生命将从中萌发。

As a child, I was very curious about this thing we call “life”. The life that is hidden in a seed, I wanted to find it, see it, expose it, touch and handle it. So I broke open, cut open, split open all kinds of seeds - wheat, peas, beans, mango, in the hope of finding some tangible evidence of life, something that looks alive, perhaps I was looking for something green, soft and juicy. But no, it was always hard, dry and woody and dead at the core.
小时候，我对所谓的“生命”感到非常好奇。那隐藏在种子里的生命，我总想把它找出来，看个究竟，让它暴露出来，去触摸和揉捏它。所以我砸开、切开、剖开各种种子——小麦、豌豆、蚕豆、芒果，希望能找到生命存在的实证，找点看起来有活气的东西，也许我是在寻找某种绿色、柔软且多汁的物质。但并非如此，种子的核心总是坚硬、干燥、木质化且毫无生气。

Yet as we all did in our first and basic experiments in science, with a bean in a glass against blotting paper, and a little water at the bottom of the glass. After a few days the seed swelled and one root emerged and made its way to the bottom of the glass, and new leaves shoot upwards, then we planted it in the soil and it grew up produced beans.
然而，就像我们初学科学时做的基础实验那样：把一颗豆子放在贴着吸水纸的玻璃杯里，杯底倒一点水。几天后，种子膨胀了，生出一条根伸向杯底，嫩叶向上挺拔。随后我们将其种入土中，它便长大结出了豆子。

This is the miracle of life - new life - resurrection. In that it is a concept that is so alien or foreign to our understanding. A seed can be a dried up hard, dead seed for centuries, bury it in the soil and the new life is released.
这就是生命的奇迹——新生命——复活。这个概念对我们的认知来说是如此陌生。一颗种子可以枯干、坚硬、如同死物一般存放几个世纪，但只要埋入土中，新生命就会释放出来。

Grains of wheat one and a half thousand years old, found in the tombs of the ancient Pharaohs were sown and germinated. This is the mystery of life in a very basic form, yet it remains a mystery. This hidden secret ingredient is there - yet we cannot unravel it.
在古法老墓中发现的具有一千五百年历史的小麦种子，播种后依然能够发芽。这是生命最基本形式的奥秘，但它依然是一个谜。这种隐藏的秘密成分就在那里，我们却无法解开它。

Will our scientists be able to create an entirely new seed in the lab, so that when it is buried in the soil, it will come to life and reproduce? I often wonder. God has already engraved into the core of every seed, a parable of the mystery of resurrection and life - life from death.
我们的科学家能在实验室里创造出一颗全新的种子，使其埋入土中后能焕发生命并繁衍吗？我经常在想。上帝早已在每一颗种子的核心处，刻下了关于复活与生命奥秘的比喻——即生命从死亡中绽放。

Therefore it is not something, that should be very hard for us to believe in, or to grasp or understand. (The joy, power, mystery of Resurrection is all around us in nature).
因此，复活并非什么难以置信、难以捉摸或难以理解的事。（复活的喜乐、力量与奥秘在自然界中俯拾皆是）。

After all we are prepared to accept as “Gospel”, far more improbable postulations and speculations of Scientists and Astronomers.
毕竟，我们甚至愿意把科学家和天文学家那些更不可思议的假设和推测奉为“真理”。

Illustration: Some years ago explorers found a Rock fragment, buried deep in the ice in Antartica. We were told that it came from Mars. How? One may ask? Volcanic activity on mars, millions of years ago hurled it into space - then it travelled trillions of miles across the solar system -and hit the earth - landing at Antartica and preserved perfectly for millions of years. And guess what - they say it contains signs of life - fossilised, microscopic organisms, millions of years old from Mars. Amazing, absolutely amazing. And we accept this as gospel, Truth, because scientists, tell us it is so!
例证：几年前，探险家在南极冰层深处发现了一块陨石碎片。我们被告知它来自火星。有人会问，这怎么可能？数百万年前火星上的火山活动将其抛入太空，随后它穿越太阳系航行了数万亿英里，撞击地球，落在南极并完美保存了数百万年。你猜怎么着——他们说其中含有生命的迹象，即来自火星的数百万年前的微型生物化石。神奇，简直不可思议。而我们接受这为真理，因为科学家告诉我们事实就是这样！

I used to be fascinated with Sci-fi films (science fiction) and often recurring theme is of alien life, they are a very superior, and scientifically advanced species with bad intentions for planet earth. There is a great interest in such possibilities, and we are actively searching for them - scanning the heavens, night and day, with highly sophisticated telescopes and electronic listening devices. There is a project called, Project SETI (Search for Extraterrestrial Intelligence). There was a Sci-fi series called The X Files, and its philosophical position is: “The truth is out there”!
我曾沉迷于科幻电影，其中经常出现的主题是外星生命，它们是某种对地球怀有敌意的高等先进物种。人们对此类可能性抱有极大兴趣，并积极寻找它们——日夜使用尖端望远镜和电子监听设备扫描星空。有一个名为“搜寻地外文明”（SETI）的项目。还有一部名为《X档案》的科幻剧，其哲学立场是：“真相就在那里”！

We are ready to believe in the existence of life forms from very primitive forms from Mars, to UFO’s, searching for the truth, out there somewhere.
我们随时准备相信各种生命形式的存在，从火星上的原始形态到不明飞行物，在遥远的某处寻找真相。

The Gospel tells us the truth is not out there somewhere - but is already here, with us. This truth has been testified to by eyewitnesses - and not based on Speculation.
福音告诉我们，真相不在遥远的某处，而就在这里，与我们同在。这一真相已有目击者作证，而非基于推测。

Those witnesses may have lived 2000 years before us, and not as scientifically or technologically advanced as we are, but by one thing they were certain, dead meant dead. And once dead, nothing came back to life. That much they knew from ancient times, as well as we know today.
那些见证人或许生活在两千年前，科技不像我们现在这样发达，但有一点他们深信不疑：死就是死了。一旦死亡，便没有生命能回来。这一点，古人知晓，正如我们今日所知。

At Lazarus’ tomb, when Jesus commanded that the stone be removed, Martha protested, (KJV) “Lord, by this time he stinketh: for he hath been dead four days.”
在拉撒路的坟墓前，当耶稣吩咐把石头挪开时，马大抗议说：“主啊，他现在必是臭了，因为他死了已经四天了。”

The Resurrection of Jesus, is not about “alien life” from another planet. Oh yes, they knew all about death, decay and its finality.
耶稣的复活并非关于来自外星的“异类生命”。哦，是的，他们完全了解死亡、腐烂及其终结性。

That is why they had such difficulty coming to terms with Jesus’ Resurrection. It cut right across all their belief systems, experience, knowledge and understanding of life and death. Such a thing was unheard of, impossible.
这正是为什么他们当时很难接受耶稣复活的事实。这完全颠覆了他们的信仰体系、经验、知识以及对生死的理解。这种事闻所未闻，绝无可能。

The women went early in the morning, with Spices to embalm a corpse. They were in deep grief, they were inconsolably sorrowful. They were shattered, when they found the empty tomb, - someone had stolen the corpse, was their immediate conclusion.
那些妇女清晨带着香料去膏抹尸体。她们沉浸在极度的悲痛中，忧伤得无法安慰。当她们发现坟墓空了时，整个人都崩溃了——她们立刻断定是有人偷走了尸体。

Mary stood outside the tomb crying. “They have taken my Lord away”, she said, and I don’t know where they have put him”. “ Sir, if you have carried him away, tell me where you have put him, and I will get him.”
马利亚站在坟墓外面哭。她说：“有人把我的主挪了去，我不知道放在哪里。”“先生，若是你把他移了去，请告诉我你把他放在哪里，我便去取他。”

They were only looking for and expecting a corpse, no more. There was no room in their world view or experience for resurrection, any more than ours.
她们寻找并预期的仅仅是一具尸体，仅此而已。在她们的世界观或经验里，复活没有容身之地，正如我们的世界观一样。

Yet something happened that changed all that, that changed all the people who encountered that reality. All we can do is to consider the documentary evidence, the witness of the Gospel accounts, and try to pick it to bits. But the reality and conviction of those witnesses is unshakable.
然而，发生了一些事情改变了这一切，改变了所有遇到那个事实的人。我们所能做的就是审视文献证据、福音书的见证，并试图挑出毛病。但那些见证人的真实感受和信念是不可动摇的。

No matter how much we dispute it, or argue with them, I don’t think we would succeed in convincing them to change their opinions.
无论我们如何质疑或与他们争论，我认为我们都无法说服他们改变主意。

    (1 John 1:1-3) That …which we have heard, which we have seen with our eyes, which we have looked at, and our hands have touched…..this we proclaim concerning the Word of Life. The life appeared; we have seen it, and testify to it (ie: bear witness to it) and we proclaim to you the eternal life, which was with the Father, and has appeared to us. We proclaim to you, what we have seen and heard, so that you also may have fellowship with us.

    （约翰一书 1:1-3） 论到从起初原有的生命之道，就是我们所听见、所看见、亲眼看过、亲手摸过的。（这生命已经显现出来，我们也看见过，现在又作见证，将原与父同在、且显现与我们那永远的生命传给你们。）我们将所看见、所听见的传给你们，使你们与我们相交。

These are the Opening Words of the first epistle of John, and they have an unshakable ring of confidence about it. The Resurrection is the sole basis, of their entire faith, belief, life and witness.
这是约翰一书的开篇语，字里行间透着不可撼动的信心。复活是他们整个信仰、信念、生命和见证的唯一根基。

The Gospels do not explain the Resurrection; the Resurrection explains the Gospels, that is why they were written. Belief in the Resurrection is not an appendage to the Christian Faith; IT IS THE CHRISTIAN FAITH.
福音书并没有解释复活；相反，是复活阐明了福音书，这正是福音书写成的原因。对复活的信仰不是基督教信仰的附属品；它本身就是基督教信仰。

If we find credible, scientific speculation, about the possibility of alien life forms, the Gospel accounts of the Resurrection of Christ, at least, deserve to be accorded that much credibility.
如果我们觉得关于外星生命可能性的科学推测是可信的，那么福音书中关于基督复活的记载，至少也值得被赋予同等的公信力。

Apart from all this there is one critical factor in all this, that should make a difference - the man Jesus is also God. Sin and death did not triumph over Jesus Christ - indeed they could not.
除此之外，还有一个关键因素能带来本质的区别——耶稣这个人同时也是神。罪恶和死亡没有战胜耶稣基督——事实上，它们也无法战胜祂。

Jesus is not subject to the laws of death and decay like we are. He is divine, deity, God, and therefore brings a new dimension that is foreign to our experience of life and death. The Power of His life over death.
耶稣不像我们一样受限于死亡和腐烂的法则。祂具有神性，祂就是神，因此祂带来了一个超越我们生死经验的新维度。这就是祂生命胜过死亡的大能。

In today’s second reading, Col. 3:1-4 Paul writes: “for you have died and your life is hidden with Christ in God.” “You have been raised to life with Christ”, Your real life is Christ.
在今天的第二篇经课中，保罗在歌罗西书 3:1-4 写道：“因为你们已经死了，你们的生命与基督一同藏在神里面。”“你们若真与基督一同复活”，你们真正的生命就是基督。

These words of St Paul refer to Christian Baptism - and are of utmost significance and comfort to the believer. New life has been given to us. We received it first at Baptism. In our Baptism we have been raised with Christ, and share in his resurrection life.
圣保罗的这些话是指基督徒的洗礼，对信徒而言具有极大的意义和安慰。新的生命已赐予我们，我们首先在洗礼中领受了它。在洗礼中，我们与基督一同复活，并分享祂复活的生命。

This is why we have the Renewal of our Baptismal Vows at the first service of Easter Day. Just like the life that is hidden in a little seed - is hidden so secure within it, yet bursts forth given the right conditions.
这就是为什么我们在复活节的首场礼拜中举行“重申洗礼誓词”。就像隐藏在小种子里的生命——它安全地隐藏在其中，一旦条件成熟就会迸发出来。

In the believer this life, begins, bursts forth into life, at their baptism. The condition for the beginning of that life.
在信徒身上，这种生命在洗礼时开始萌发并绽放。那是生命开始的契机。

The celebration of the great mysteries of our faith, the passion, death and resurrection of our Lord, these are not EXTERNAL TRUTHS to be celebrated once a year. They are truths which have to affect our lives here and now. Because He lives, I live.
我们对信仰伟大奥秘的庆贺——即主受难、受死与复活——并非每年庆祝一次的“外部真理”。它们是必须影响我们此时此刻生活的真理。因祂活着，我也活着。

Our Lord’s resurrection from the dead is the foundation of our Christian faith. It is God’s assurance to us that we will never die, and never be ultimately defeated.
我主从死里复活是我们基督信仰的基石。这是上帝给我们的保证：我们永不会灭亡，也永不会遭遇最终的失败。

This is what made St Paul say, “I now live, not I. but Christ lives in me. (Gal 2:20)
这正是圣保罗所说的：“现在活着的不再是我，乃是基督在我里面活着。”（加拉太书 2:20）

We must not think of Christ rising from the dead, and then leaving us to cope as best as we can. No he remains with us, present always, unseen to human eyes, and beyond the touch of the hand.
我们不可认为基督从死里复活后就丢下我们，让我们自顾自地应对生活。不，祂常与我们同在，虽为人眼所不见、人手所不触，却时刻临在。

Through Faith, we come to realise more and more his presence within us and around us.
通过信心，我们越来越能体会到祂在我们里面以及在我们周围的临在。

CHRIST IS RISEN! He is risen indeed! Alleluia!
基督复活了！祂果然复活了！哈利路亚！
