# 棕树节主日讲章：和散那，拯救我们

# palm Sunday Sermon: Hosanna, Save Us Now
## 经文：马太福音 21:1-11
## Scripture: Matthew 21:1-11

耶稣和门徒将近耶路撒冷，来到伯法其，在橄榄山那里。耶稣就打发两个门徒，对他们说：“你们往对面村子里去，必看见一匹驴拴在那里，还有驴驹同在一处；你们解开，牵到我这里来。若有人对你们说什么，你们就说：‘主要用它。’那人必立时让你们牵来。”
As they approached Jerusalem and came to Bethphage on the Mount of Olives, Jesus sent two disciples, saying to them, “Go to the village ahead of you, and at once you will find a donkey tied there, with her colt by her. Untie them and bring them to me. If anyone says anything to you, say that the Lord needs them, and he will send them right away.”

这事修成是要应验先知的话，说：要对锡安的居民（原文是女子）说：看哪，你的王来到你这里，是温柔的，又骑着驴，就是骑着驴驹子。
This took place to fulfill what was spoken through the prophet: “Say to Daughter Zion, ‘See, your king comes to you, gentle and riding on a donkey, and on a colt, the foal of a donkey.’”

门徒就照耶稣所吩咐的去行，牵了驴和驴驹来，把自己的衣服搭在上面，耶稣就骑上。众人多半把衣服铺在路上；还有人砍下树枝来铺在路上。
The disciples went and did as Jesus had instructed them. They brought the donkey and the colt and placed their cloaks on them for Jesus to sit on. A very large crowd spread their cloaks on the road, while others cut branches from the trees and spread them on the road.

前行后随的众人喊着说：和散那（原有求救的意思，在此乃是称颂的话）归于大卫的子孙！奉主名来的是应当称颂的！高高在上和散那！
The crowds that went ahead of him and those that followed shouted, “Hosanna to the Son of David!” “Blessed is he who comes in the name of the Lord!” “Hosanna in the highest heaven!”

耶稣既进了耶路撒冷，合城都惊动了，说：“这是谁？”众人说：“这是加利利拿撒勒的先知耶稣。”
When Jesus entered Jerusalem, the whole city was stirred and asked, “Who is this?” The crowds answered, “This is Jesus, the prophet from Nazareth in Galilee.”
讲章内容 / Sermon Content

又是一年的棕树节，当我们纪念耶稣骑着小驴而非高大的战马进入耶路撒冷时，我们被提醒：耶稣是和平之君。祂的国度建立在谦卑与属灵之上，而非政治与军事力量，亦非肉体的强壮与野心勃勃的侵略。
Another year, another Palm Sunday, while we remember Jesus’ entry into Jerusalem on a small donkey, not a giant warhorse, thus we are reminded Jesus as the prince of peace, his kingdom is based on humility and spirituality, not on political and military power, not with physical strength and ambitious aggression.

耶稣正走向祂的审判，那是因爱罪人而受的审判。耶稣的目标是和好——创造主与受造物之间的和好，竞争对手与仇敌之间的和好……耶稣要求我们跟随祂，作使人和睦的人。耶稣向我们展示了如何通过沉默、闭口不言来避免冲突和争论。
Jesus was heading to his sentence, his sentence for loving the sinful human beings. Jesus aimed for reconciliation, reconciliation between the Creator and his creation, reconciliation between rivalries and enemies…. Jesus asked us to follow him and be peacemakers. Jesus showed us how to avoid conflicts and arguments by shutting up, by keeping silent.

不需要战马。但现今却有坦克和战机，有炸弹和导弹。想想一战、二战、中日战争、朝鲜战争、越南战争、伊拉克战争、乌克兰战争、伊朗战争……等等。战争如此激烈，以至于谈判失效，国家间的斡旋无法调解，外交努力也毫无建树。
No warhorses needed. But there are tanks and war planes. There are bombs and missiles. Think about the 1st and 2nd world wars, the Sino Japanese war, the Korean war, the Vietnam war, Iraq war, Ukraine war, Iran war ….so on and so forth. Wars have been so intense that negotiations don’t work, go between countries can’t mediate, diplomatic efforts are not achieving anything.

先知撒迦利亚预言一位仆人君王将要来临，以祂慈爱的服务施行治理。撒迦利亚谈到一位永不压迫、永不下令攻击的王……而是带来拯救与医治。撒迦利亚书第9章说：“看哪，你的王来到你这里！他是公义的，并且施行拯救，谦谦和和地骑着驴，就是骑着驴的驹子。”撒迦利亚继续写道：这位王必除灭战车、战马、争战的弓、争战的箭和兵器……这位王只支持并允许仁慈与爱的治理，没有独裁，没有暴政。
Prophet Zechariah promised a Servant King would come to rule with his loving service. Zechariah talked about a King who would never oppress, never give commands to attack… but to bring salvation and deliverance. Zechariah chapter 9: "see your king comes to you, righteous and having salvation, gentle and riding on a donkey, on a colt, the foal of a donkey..." Zechariah continues: this king will take away the chariots, the war horses, the battle bow, the battle arrows, the battle weapons… this king, endorse and allows only rules of mercy, rules of love, no rules of dictatorship, no rules of tyranny.

你可能会问，那该如何消除暴政和独裁？有些人尝试抗议和骚乱，有些人尝试制裁、关税、战争、攻击和暗杀。他们尝试了一切，但为什么不试试耶稣呢？为什么不更经常、更认真地尝试耶稣的方式呢？
Then how to remove tyranny and dictatorship, you may ask. Some people try protests and riots. Some people try sanctions, tariffs, wars, attacks, assassinations. They try everything but why don’t they try Jesus, why don’t they try Jesus more often and more seriously.

几个世纪以来，世界各地的人们一直遭受着政治动荡、民族主义和殖民主义的痛苦。耶稣时代的犹太人，神自己的选民，正被一个异教帝国殖民。罗马人不尊重犹太宗教制度，以重税剥削他们，占领圣地。犹太人受到压迫和镇压，他们渴望战斗却无能为力，内心充满强烈的情感，深切渴望有人能为他们战斗，恢复他们的政治自由和宗教自治。
People over the centuries, everywhere of the world, have been suffering from political turmoils, from nationalism, from colonialism. The Jewish people, God’s own chosen people in Jesus’ time was colonized by a pagan empire. The Romans disrespected the Jewish religious system, they exploited the Jews with heavy taxation, they occupied the holy land, the Jews were oppressed and suppressed, they wanted to fight but incapable to fight, they always fill with a intense emotions and a deep longing to have someone to fight for them, to restore their political freedom and religious autonomy.

群众大声呼喊“和散那”（求你现在拯救）。他们相信耶稣是大卫的子孙，是应许的弥赛亚，但他们对上帝的方式感到困惑和不确定，正如我们现在依然对上帝的方式感到困惑一样……我们相信力量，人类的力量；我们相信智能，人工智能……我们相信技术，甚至想用技术取代上帝。
The crowd shouted aloud Hosanna "Save us now," They believed Jesus as the Son of David, as the promised Messiah, but they were confused and unsure of God’s way, as we are now confused still of God’s way… we believe in power, human power, we believe in intelligence, artificial intelligence... we believe in technology, even technology to replace God.

我们忘记了上帝的话……诗篇 118:8-9 “投靠耶和华，强似倚赖人；投靠耶和华，强似倚赖王子。”诗篇 20:7 “有人靠车，有人靠马，但我们要提到耶和华我们神的名。”上帝为我们争战……耶稣通过在十字架上的死获得了胜利。我们赢得任何战斗，不是倚靠势力，不是倚靠才能，乃是倚靠我的灵（撒迦利亚书 4:6）。我们要奉我们神的名竖立旌旗……我们的主是好牧人，祂为羊群争战，羊群不需要亲自对抗豺狼和强盗，只需跟随牧人、信任牧人，因为好牧人为羊舍命。
We forgot God’s own word… Psalm 118: 8-9 It is better to take refuge in the Lord than to trust in man. It is better to take refuge in the Lord than to trust in princes. Psalm 20: 7 ‘Some trust in chariots and some in horses, but we trust in the name of the Lord our God… God fights the battles for us… Jesus is victorious through his death on the cross, we will win any battles, not by might, nor by power, but by my Spirit says the Lord Almighty (Zechariah 4:6) and we will lift up our banners in the name of the Lord… Our Lord is a good shepherd, he fights the battles for his sheep, his sheep needed not to fight the wolves and the robbers themselves, just follow the shepherd and trust the shepherd, as a good shepherd lays down his life for the sheep.

我们多久祈祷一次：主啊，救救我们？救我们脱离家庭危机，救我们脱离疾病。当我们在自尊中挣扎、在成瘾中挣扎、在慢性疼痛中挣扎、在癌症中挣扎时，我们是否祈求上帝的拯救？
How often do we pray: save us Lord. Save us from our family crisis, save us from our ill health and diseases. Do we ask God to save us when we battle with our self esteems, when we battle with addictions, when we battle with chronic pains, when we battle with cancer.

几年前，我曾与突发性血压下降和严重的血液感染作斗争……那是九年前 2017 年的棕树节前夕。我在周五被救护车紧急送往 Box Hill 急诊室，接受了大量的治疗，插满了输送抗生素的管子。周五后的第三天我病得很重，那天正好是 2017 年的棕树节……我的丈夫 Rick 和两个儿子以为要失去我了，他们一起痛哭祷告……我在 St James Ivanhoe 的教区教友们也日夜为我禁食祷告。当时我身体非常虚弱，但灵里却非常警醒，我在心里向主呼喊：主啊，请救救我，和散那归于得胜的君王和全能的医治者。请救救我，让我的家人和教会不再忧伤；请救救我，让我能活得长久来事奉神、服事教会。
Years ago I battled with a sudden drop of blood pressure, a severe blood infection … I was rushed to Box Hill emergency in an ambulance on a Friday, I undergone lots of treatments with needles and tubes of antibiotics and more antibiotics, I was very sick on the third day from Friday, which was exactly 9 years ago on Palm Sunday 2017… my husband Rick and my two boys thought they were losing me, they cried together and they prayed together… my parish at St James Ivanhoe fasted and prayed for me day and night… I was very weak physically but I was very alert spiritually, in my heart I shouted to the Lord: please save me Lord, Hosanna to the triumphant king and the able healer, please save me so that my family and my parish won’t be so sad… please God to save me so I may live longer to serve God and to serve the church.

在那个棕树节，在那周受难周期间，许多人与我一同争战，凭着十足的灵力，靠着上帝神迹般的怜悯和大能，通过敬业的医疗团队，对抗我那神秘而突发的疾病。随后，在一些有效的抗生素治疗下，我渐渐好转。抗生素冲洗并清除了我血液中所有的细菌。我在洗脚礼礼拜四（Maundy Thursday）康复出院，在受难日和复活节主日居家休息。虽然我无法参加教会的崇拜，但赞美和感激之情已从我心中满溢。
On that Palm Sunday, during that Holy Week, many people battled with me, to fight my mysterious and sudden illness, with full spiritual strength, by God’s miraculous mercy and power, through the dedicated medical team. I was then got better and better with some effective antibiotics, the antibiotics flushed and cleared all the bacteria in my blood, I was well enough to be discharged on Maundy Thursday, I rested at home on Good Friday and Easter Sunday…I was not able to attend church Services, but the praises and gratitude overflowed from my heart.

我们的上帝是伟大的神。让我们敬拜基督，永远向祂欢唱和散那。让我们随耶稣进入圣周：
Our God is a awesome God. Let us worship Christ and sing Hosanna to him always. Let us enter into the Holy Week with Jesus.

    第一天 棕树节主日：我们挥动手中的枝子，欢迎耶稣进入我们的生命。
    Day 1 Palm Sunday: we wave and welcome Jesus into our life.

    第二天 圣周一：我们求耶稣洁净我们的心，正如祂洁净圣殿一样。
    Day 2 Holy Monday: we ask Jesus to cleanse our hearts as he cleansed the temple.

    第三天 圣周二：我们自我省察，是否在圣灵里为基督结出好果子。
    Day 3 Holy Tuesday: we examine ourselves, whether we are in the Spirit, to bear some good fruit for Christ.

    第四天 圣周三：我们承认，自己并未一直用最好的来膏抹耶稣。
    Day 4 Holy Wednesday: we confess we have not been anointing Jesus with our best.

    第五天 圣周四：我们必须学习并重新学习“彼此相爱”的新命令。
    Day 5 Holy Thursday: we must learn and re-learn the New Commandment of love.

    第六天 受难日：省察并自问：当耶稣被钉十字架时，我们在场吗？
    Day 6 Good Friday: check and ask ourself: were we there when Jesus was crucified?

    第七天 圣周六：我们感谢耶稣为我们降入死亡……祂为复活做了准备，因此我们在七日的头一日以新的循环庆祝复活节。
    Day 7 Holy Saturday: We thank Jesus for descending into death on our behalf … He prepared himself for resurrection, so we are celebrating Easter in a new cycle on the first day of the week.

我相信通过基督，你们都是上帝亲爱的孩子。愿上帝在 2026 年受难周期间降福给祂所有的圣徒。奉耶稣的名，阿们。
I believe you are all God’s beloved children through Christ. May God bless all his holy ones during the Holy Week 2026. For Jesus’ sake. Amen.
