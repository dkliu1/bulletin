# OSL Bentleigh Chapter Healing Service  
# OSL 本特利分会医治聚会

**15 March 2026 Sunday 2pm at St David’s**  
2026年3月15日 周日 下午2点  
地点：St David’s 教会

Speaker: Rev Roger Featherston  
讲员：Roger Featherston 牧师

---

The Order of St Luke the Physician Healing Ministries (OSL)  
is a worldwide interdenominational Christian ministry.  

圣路加医治事工（OSL）是一个全球性的跨宗派基督徒团体。

Both clergy and lay members believe that healing of body and mind  
is an important part of the ministry of Jesus Christ.

无论牧者还是信徒都相信：  
身体与心灵的医治是耶稣基督事工的重要部分。

OSL believes that Jesus commissioned His followers  
to continue this ministry according to the New Testament.

OSL 也相信耶稣差遣祂的门徒，  
按照新约的教导继续这项医治的事工。

> “Jesus went through all the towns and villages, teaching in their synagogues,  
> preaching the good news of the kingdom and healing every disease and sickness.”  
> Matthew 9:35–36  

> “耶稣走遍各城各乡，在会堂里教训人，  
> 宣讲天国的福音，并医治各样的病症。”  
> 马太福音 9:35–36

The ultimate aim of the Order of St Luke  
is to restore healing ministry to the normal life of the church.

圣路加医治团契的最终目标，  
是让医治的事工重新成为教会生活的常态。
