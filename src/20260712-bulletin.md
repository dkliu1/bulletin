REFLECTION / 默想
Matt 13:1-9; 18-23 / 马太福音 13:1-9; 18-23

Jesus' parable of the sower reminds us that our calling is not to judge the soil but to faithfully sow the seed of God's Word. Like the generous sower, God scatters His grace freely, and it is He alone who gives the growth. We may plant, and others may water, but the harvest belongs to God. This week, look for simple opportunities to share Christ's love through kindness, friendship, hospitality, or an invitation to church or our parish café. Don't worry about the results—simply sow generously and trust God to bring forth a harvest in His perfect time.

耶稣关于撒种的比喻提醒我们，我们的呼召不是去审判土壤，而是忠实地撒下神话语的种子。正如那位慷慨的撒种者一样，神自由地散播祂的恩典，且唯有祂使之生长。我们可能负责栽种，其他人可能负责浇灌，但收成属于神。本周，请寻找简单的机会，通过仁慈、友谊、款待，或者邀请他人来教堂或我们的教区咖啡馆，来分享基督的爱。不要担心结果——只需慷慨地撒种，并信靠神在祂完美的时刻带来收成。
PARISH PRAYER FOR GROWTH / 教区成长祷文

God of mission,
who alone brings growth to your church,
send your Holy Spirit to give Vision to our planning,
Wisdom to our actions, Joy to our worship, and power to our witness
Help our church to grow in numbers, in spiritual commitment to you,
And in service to our local community,
through Jesus Christ our Lord. Amen

以及对本地社区的服务里不断增长，
奉靠我们的主耶稣基督。阿们。
