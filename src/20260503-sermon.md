约翰福音 14 章 / John 14

“你们心里不要忧愁；你们信神，也当信我。在我父的家里有许多住处；若是没有，我就早已告诉你们了。我去原是为你们预备地方去。我若去为你们预备了地方，就必再来接你们到我那里去，我在哪里，叫你们也在那里。我往哪里去，你们知道那条路。”
“Do not let your hearts be troubled. You believe in God; believe also in me. My Father’s house has many rooms; if that were not so, would I have told you that I am going there to prepare a place for you? And if I go and prepare a place for you, I will come back and take you to be with me that you also may be where I am. You know the way to the place where I am going.”

多马对他说：“主啊，我们不知道你往哪里去，怎么知道那条路呢？”耶稣说：“我就是道路、真理、生命；若不借着我，没有人能到父那里去。你们若认识我，也就认识我的父。从今以后，你们认识他，并且已经看见他。”
Thomas said to him, “Lord, we don’t know where you are going, so how can we know the way?” Jesus answered, “I am the way and the truth and the life. No one comes to the Father except through me. If you really know me, you will know my Father as well. From now on, you do know him and have seen him.”

腓力对他说：“求主将父显给我们看，我们就知足了。”耶稣对他说：“腓力，我与你们同在这样长久，你还不认识我吗？人看见了我，就是看见了父；你怎么说‘将父显给我们看’呢？
Philip said, “Lord, show us the Father and that will be enough for us.” Jesus answered: “Don’t you know me, Philip, even after I have been among you such a long time? Anyone who has seen me has seen the Father. How can you say, ‘Show us the Father’?

我在父里面，父在我里面，你不信吗？我对你们所说的话，不是凭着自己说的，乃是住在我里面的父作他自己的事。你们当信我，我在父里面，父在我里面；即或不信，也当因我所作的事信我。
Don’t you believe that I am in the Father, and that the Father is in me? The words I say to you I do not speak on my own authority. Rather, it is the Father, living in me, who is doing his work. Believe me when I say that I am in the Father and the Father is in me; or at least believe on the evidence of the works themselves.

我实实在在地告诉你们，我所作的事，信我的人也要作，并且要作比这更大的事，因为我往父那里去。你们奉我的名无论求什么，我必成就，叫父因儿子得荣耀。你们若奉我的名求什么，我必成就。”
Very truly I tell you, whoever believes in me will do the works I have been doing, and they will do even greater things than these, because I am going to the Father. And I will do whatever you ask in my name, so that the Father may be glorified in the Son. You may ask me for anything in my name, and I will do it.
牧者感言 / Pastor's Reflections

你们心里不要忧愁，不要因为任何人或任何事忧愁。但我们常有忧愁，我们很容易忧愁，这就是为什么我们来到教会寻求平安，寻求指引。这就是为什么教会应当是一个受欢迎的教会，一个关怀的教会，应当提供空间和安慰，让人进来寻求忧愁的解脱，而不是进入一个充满麻烦的地方——一个被财务、纠纷、流言蜚语或臃肿的管理架构所困扰的地方。
Do not let your hearts be troubled, do not let your hearts be troubled… for anyone or for any matters. But we are troubled, we are easily troubled, that is why we come to church to seek peace, to seek guidance. That is why a church should be a welcoming church, should be a caring church, should provide space and comfort, for people to come in to find relief from their troubles, but not to come in a troubled place, a place troubled with finance, with disputes, with gossips or some overwhelming complication in structures and management.

最近我给教区、给教堂管理员以及几位目睹了车祸、受损的教堂建筑以及他们代理牧师无助模样的教友带来了一些麻烦。我再次感谢所有安慰我、帮助我、倾听我并相信我的人。我特别感谢 Ross，因为这次糟糕的事故，他承担了许多额外的工作……我感谢他，也感谢你们中的许多人站在我身边支持我，相信我在整件事中是清白的。
Recently I caused some troubles for the parish, for the wardens and for a few people who saw the crashed car, the damaged church building, and the helplessness of their acting vicar. Once again I thank all those who comforted me, assisted me, listened to me and believed in me. I thank particularly Ross, who has been doing a lot of extra work because of this nasty accident… I thank him and many of you for standing by me and backing me up, believing I was rather innocent in the whole matter.

请不要再为这件事心里忧愁。一切都过去了。显然，神希望我们体验祂的爱、祂的同在和祂的力量，这力量每天盘旋并覆盖着 Moorabbin 的圣大卫教堂，祂保护的手触及并拯救在每个角落的每一个人。
Please do not let your hearts be troubled anymore over this incident. It is all over. It is all clear that God likes us to experience his love, his presence and his power, which hovers and covers St David’s Moorabbin everyday, his protecting hands reach and rescue every person, in every corner.

门徒们当时忧愁，因为他们不确定未来：真的有天堂吗？天上真的有一位父亲吗？天堂在哪里？谁是那位父亲？如果我的会众不知道这些答案，我会忧愁。如果人们拒绝天堂，拒绝天父，我会更加忧愁。
The disciples were troubled as they were not sure about their future, is it really a heaven? Is it really a father in heaven? where is the heaven and who is the father? I will be troubled, if my parishioners didn’t know the answers. I will be more troubled if people refused the heaven, and refused the father.

耶稣解释说，在他父的家里有许多“房间”。希腊语中“房间”一词（μοναί）也可以翻译为“住处”。“住处”一词具有更深刻的影响力，它传达了一种永久感。当耶稣再来迎接门徒和所有信徒进入天堂时，他们将与父和子永远同住。虽然子在肉眼看来只是暂时离开，直到祂再来，但他们在乐园里的地位将永无止境。
Jesus explains that there are many rooms in His Father’s house. The Greek word for rooms, μοναί, can also be translated “dwelling places.” “Dwelling places” has a more impactful meaning. It conveys a sense of permanence. When Jesus returns to take the disciples and all believers to heaven, they will dwell with the Father and the Son eternally. While the Son will be visibly absent for only a little while until His return, their place in paradise will have no end.

耶稣声明，若不借着祂，没有人能到父那里去。希腊语中“借着”一词（διά）也可以翻译为“因为”或“由于”。当我们考虑到只有通过耶稣的生命、死亡和复活才能亲近天父时，这两个替代译法就非常有意义了。没有基督，天堂的大门是关闭的。通过对基督所成之事的信心，我们确信在天堂都有一个地方，并且我们都能体验到天父的供应和保护。
Jesus states that no one comes to the Father except through Him. The Greek word for through, διά, can also be translated “on account of” or “by reason of.” These two alternate translations make sense when we consider that there is access to the Father only because of Jesus’ life, death, and resurrection. Without Christ, the doors of heaven are closed. Through faith in what Christ has done, we are sure we all have a place in heaven, and we can all experience God’s provision and protection as our heavenly father.

对于犹太人来说，亲近父是最重要的。耶稣想要让祂的门徒确信，他们不仅要借着祂才能亲近父，而且拥有了子也就拥有了父。认识子就是认识父。通过说“你们信神，也当信我”，即便在观察了祂三年的教导、成就预言和施行神迹后，一些门徒仍然在“耶稣就是神”这一真理中挣扎。耶稣是在说祂就是神。耶稣在说：来吧，你们既然信神，那就信我。
For Jews, reaching the Father was of utmost importance. Jesus wants to convince His disciples that not only is it through Him that they will reach the Father, but that to have the Son is to have the Father also. To know the Son is to know the Father. By saying, “Believe in God; believe also in Me,” Even after watching Him teach, fulfill prophecies, and perform miracles for three years, some of the disciples still struggle with the truth that Jesus is God. Jesus is saying He is God. Jesus is saying, come on, you believe in God, then you believe in me.

腓力对耶稣说：“主啊，将父显给我们看，我们就知足了”（14:8）。耶稣说：“腓力，我与你们同在这样长久，你还不认识我吗？人看见了我，就是看见了父”（14:9）。这里耶稣呼应了约翰福音序言中的肯定：“从来没有人看见神，只有在父怀里的独生子将他表明出来”（1:18）。
Philip said to Jesus: “Lord, show us the Father, and we will be satisfied” (14:8). Jesus said “Have I been with you all this time, Philip, and you still do not know me? Whoever has seen me has seen the Father” (14:9). Here Jesus echoes an affirmation from the prologue of John’s Gospel: “No one has ever seen God. It is God the only Son, who is close to the Father’s heart, who has made him known” (1:18).

这就是耶稣全部的使命：让人认识父，揭示神是谁。耶稣从父的家里来，现在正要回到那里去，祂是神的人格和神性的最充分启示。如果我们想知道神是谁，只需看耶稣。耶稣所说的一切话，所作的一切事，都来自神，并向我们展示了神是谁（14:10-11）。
This is the whole of Jesus’ mission, to make known the Father, to reveal who God is. Jesus, who has come from the house of the Father and is now returning there, is the fullest revelation of the person and character of God. If we want to know who God is, we need look no further than Jesus. All the words that Jesus has spoken, all the works that he has done, come from God and show us who God is (14:10-11).

耶稣传达出祂要离开的信息，但不要担心，不要因为帮助和希望消失而忧愁。耶稣会回来的。耶稣离开门徒只有两个充分的理由：一是为他们和我们在天堂预备地方；二是为圣灵的降临预备空间，让圣灵的力量坚固我们，圣灵的智慧指引我们，圣灵的鼓励安慰我们。
Jesus communicates that He is going away, but don’t worry, don’t be troubled that help and hope is gone. Jesus will return. Jesus is leaving the disciples only for two good reasons. One reason is to prepare a place in heaven for them and for us. The other reason is to prepare a place for the Holy Spirit to come, for the power of the Holy Spirit to strengthen us, for the wisdom of the Holy Spirit to counsel us, and for the encouragement of the Holy Spirit to comfort us.

耶稣宣告说：“我就是道路、真理、生命；若不借着我，没有人能到父那里去。你们若认识我，也就认识我的父。”
Jesus proclaimed, “I am the way and the truth and the life. No one comes to the Father except through me. 7 If you really know me, you will know my Father as well.

“道路”这个词字面上是指“路途、路径”……将我们从一个地方带到另一个地方，将我们从极度污秽之处带到极其神圣荣耀之处。
The word “way” is literally a “road, a path….. getting us from one place to another, getting us from somewhere extremely filthy to somewhere extremely holy and glorious.

耶稣说：我就是那唯一的道路。
Jesus says I am THE way.

只有一条路径。通往天堂只有一条路，那就是通过耶稣基督。耶稣是道路，因为祂是真理，因为祂是生命，“若不借着他，没有人能到父那里去”。
There’s only one path. There’s only one way to heaven and it’s through Jesus Christ. Jesus is the way because he’s the truth, because he’s the life and “no one comes to the Father except through him.”

耶稣极其明确地表示，祂是通往天堂的唯一道路。祂是通往神的唯一道路。没有备选方案。救赎唯独通过祂。耶稣告诉多马和我们所有人，为了进入天堂，我们究竟必须做什么：信靠祂。信任祂。将我们自己交托给祂。为什么？因为祂是道路、真理、生命，若不借着祂，没有人能来到父面前，来到父的宝座前，来到父的家里。
Jesus makes it abundantly clear that he is the only way to heaven. He is the only way to God. There is no plan B. Salvation comes exclusively through him. Jesus tells Thomas and all of us exactly what we must do in order to get to heaven: Believe on him. Trust in him. Turn ourselves over to him. Why? Because he is the way the truth and the life and no one comes to the Father, to the father’s throne, to the father’s house.. except through him.

耶稣一直是极其诚实、透明、坦率且直接的。你们心里不要忧愁。信神，信耶稣，信圣灵。
Jesus has been utterly honest, transparent, forthright, and straight forward. Do not let your hearts be troubled. Believe in God, believe in Jesus, believe in the Holy Spirit.

当我们相信时，我们就作耶稣所作的事，服侍耶稣所服侍的人。耶稣现在已回到父那里，圣灵赋能我们去作更好、更多、更大的事。耶稣承诺：“你们奉我的名无论求什么，我必成就……你们若奉我的名求什么，我必成就。”
As we believe, we do what Jesus do, we serve whom Jesus serve. Jesus is now gone back to the Father, the Holy Spirit empowers us to do even better and more and greater. Jesus promised, ‘And I will do whatever you ask in my name.. you may ask me for anything in my name, and I will do it.’

耶稣会求父的旨意成就，求父的名得荣耀。在耶稣的名里，我们像耶稣那样祈求。当我们按照耶稣的心意祈求时，耶稣必成就我们所愿。
Jesus would ask that the Father’s will to be done, the Father’s name being glorified. In Jesus’ name we ask as Jesus ask. When we ask according to Jesus’ desire, Jesus will do what we desire.

我们渴望的是一个敬虔的教会吗？还是渴望一个大型教会？我们渴望的是一个让神喜悦的教会吗？还是渴望一个让每个人都开心的教会？
Do we desire a godly church? Or do we desire a mega church? Do we desire a church which makes God happy? Or do we desire a church which makes everybody happy?

如果我们无法理清这个简单的优先级，我会感到忧愁。但我知道我不必忧愁，因为我有巨大的信心，深信你们在圣大卫教堂的所有人，将与新牧师一起，为神做正确的事。赞美主。阿门。
I will be troubled if we can’t get this simple priority right. But I know I won’t need to be troubled, because I have great faith and confidence that you all at St David’s, together with the new vicar, will be making things right for God. Praise the Lord. Amen.
