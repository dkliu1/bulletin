让我们祷告
Let Us Pray

丰盛的上帝，求你赐给本堂一位忠心的牧者，忠实宣讲你的话语，施行你的圣礼；成为鼓励者，装备你的子民参与事奉，使我们得以完成你的呼召。也求你赐智慧、分辨力与耐心给拣选的人，并赐我们温暖慷慨的心。奉基督的名祷告，阿们。
Bountiful God, give to this parish a faithful pastor who will faithfully speak your word and minister your sacraments; an encourager who will equip your people for ministry and enable us to fulfil our calling. Give to those who choose, wisdom, discernment and patience, and to us give warm and generous hearts, for Christ’s sake. Amen.

生日祷告
A Birthday Prayer

愿上帝的平安在你生日这一天与你同在；愿祂丰盛的恩典环绕你，带领你行在祂的道路中。愿祂的祝福常与你同在，赐你智慧，在你生日以及人生每一天、每一处看顾你。愿圣父、圣子、圣灵保护你，并天天供应你。阿们。
May the peace of God be with you on your Birthday. May His gifts of grace surround you as you follow in His way. May His blessings rest upon you, and His wisdom guide you on your birth date and always in all places. May God the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit protect you and provide for you every day and in every way. Amen.
