## REFLECTIONS ON HOLY WEEK
## 圣周默想

Holy Week stands as the pinnacle of the Christian calendar.
圣周是基督教历中最重要的时刻。

It is a sacred time to thank Jesus for His journey to the cross.
这是我们为主耶稣走向十字架而献上感恩的神圣时刻。

We thank God for His plan of redemption through Jesus’ life, death, and resurrection.
感谢神借着耶稣的生命、死亡与复活成就救赎计划。

Holy Week is both a historical remembrance and a spiritual encounter.
圣周既是历史的纪念，也是属灵的经历。

When we attend services and spend quiet time with God,
当我们参与礼拜并安静在神面前时，

we experience Christ’s love, sacrifice, and victory.
我们会体会基督的爱、牺牲与得胜。

Let us trust and worship Him every day.
让我们每天信靠并敬拜祂。

Even in difficulties, we have comfort, hope, and assurance in the risen Christ.
即使面对困难，我们在复活的基督里仍有安慰、盼望与确据。
