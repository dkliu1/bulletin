# 使徒行传与圣灵降临节的恩典 / Acts and the Grace of Pentecost
# 使徒行传 2:2-21 / Acts 2:2-21

五旬节到了，门徒都聚集在一处。
When the day of Pentecost came, they were all together in one place.

忽然，从天上有响声下来，好像一阵大风吹过，充满了他们所坐的屋子；
Suddenly a sound like the blowing of a violent wind came from heaven and filled the whole house where they were sitting.

又有舌头如火焰显现出来，分开落在他们各人头上。
They saw what seemed to be tongues of fire that separated and came to rest on each of them.

他们就都被圣灵充满，按着圣灵所赐的口才说起别国的话来。
All of them were filled with the Holy Spirit and began to speak in other tongues as the Spirit enabled them.

那时，有虔诚的犹太人从天下各国来，住在耶路撒冷。
Now there were staying in Jerusalem God-fearing Jews from every nation under heaven.

这声音一响，众人都来聚集，各人听见门徒用众人的乡谈说话，就甚纳闷，
When they heard this sound, a crowd came together in bewilderment, because each one heard their own language being spoken.

都惊讶希奇说：“看哪，这说话的不都是加利利人吗？
Utterly amazed, they asked: “Aren’t all these who are speaking Galileans?

我们各人怎么听见他们说我们生来所谈的乡谈呢？
Then how is it that each of us hears them in our native language?

我们帕提亚人、玛代人、以拦人，和住在美索不达米亚、犹太、加帕皇家、本都、亚细亚、
Parthians, Medes and Elamites; residents of Mesopotamia, Judea and Cappadocia, Pontus and Asia,

弗吕家、旁非利亚、埃及的人，并靠近古利奈的利比亚一带地方的人，从罗马来的客旅中，或是犹太人，或是入犹太教的人，
Phrygia and Pamphylia, Egypt and the parts of Libya near Cyrene; visitors from Rome (both Jews and converts to Judaism);

克里特人和阿拉伯人，都听见他们用我们的乡谈，讲说神的大作为。”
Cretans and Arabs—we hear them declaring the wonders of God in our own tongues!”

众人就都惊奇猜疑，彼此说：“这是什么意思呢？”
Amazed and perplexed, they asked one another, “What does this mean?”

还有人讥诮说：“他们无非是新酒灌满了。”
Some, however, made fun of them and said, “They have had too much wine.”

彼得和十一个使徒站起来，高声说：“犹太人和一切住在耶路撒冷的人哪，这件事你们当知道，也当侧耳听我的话。
Then Peter stood up with the Eleven, raised his voice and addressed the crowd: “Fellow Jews and all of you who live in Jerusalem, let me explain this to you; listen carefully to what I say.

你们想这些人是醉了？其实不是醉了，因为时候刚到巳初。
These people are not drunk, as you suppose. It’s only nine in the morning!

这正是先知约珥所说的：
No, this is what was spoken by the prophet Joel:

‘神说：在末后的日子，我要将我的灵浇灌凡有血气的。你们的儿女要说预言；你们的少年人要见异象；老年人要做异梦。
“‘In the last days, God says, I will pour out my Spirit on all people. Your sons and daughters will prophesy, your young men will see visions, your old men will dream dreams.

在那些日子，我要将我的灵浇灌我的仆人和婢女，他们就要说预言。
Even on my servants, both men and women, I will pour out my Spirit in those days, and they will prophesy.

在天上我要显出奇事；在地下我要显出神迹；有血，有火，有烟雾。
I will show wonders in the heavens above and signs on the earth below, blood and fire and billows of smoke.

日头要变为黑暗，月亮要变为血；这都在主大而明显的日子未到以前。
The sun will be turned to darkness and the moon to blood before the coming of the great and glorious day of the Lord.

到那时候，凡求告主名的，就必得救。’
And everyone who calls on the name of the Lord will be saved.’
## 救赎与圣灵的工 / Salvation and the Work of the Holy Spirit

我们都知道：凡求告主名的就必得救——从我们的罪恶中得救，从永恒的死亡中得救，从与神及我们所爱之人永远的分离中得救。如果我们渴望得救，我们就需要求告救主主耶稣基督的名；但若没有圣灵的帮助，没有人能称耶稣为“主”。哥林多前书 12:3 中圣保罗说：“若不是被圣灵感动的，也没有能说耶稣是主的。”
We all know: everyone who calls on the name of the Lord will be saved, saved from our sins, saved from our eternal death, saved from forever separation from God and our loved ones. If we like to be saved, then we need to call on the name of the Saviour Lord Jesus Christ, but no one will be able to call Jesus ‘Lord’, without the help of the Holy Spirit, 1 Corinthians 12:3 St Paul says ‘no one can say Jesus is Lord, except by the Holy Spirit.’

圣灵职分至关紧要的重要性，在马太福音 12:31-32 和马可福音 3:28-30 中可以进一步看到。耶稣说：“所以我告诉你们：人一切的罪和亵渎的话，都可得赦免；惟独亵渎圣灵，总不得赦免。凡说话干犯人子的，还可得赦免；惟独说话干犯圣灵的，今世、来世总不得赦免。” 圣经表明，对圣灵不敬、粗暴和悖逆是“不可饶恕的罪……永恒的罪……这需要受到永远的刑罚”。
The utmost importance of the role of the Holy Spirit can further to be seen in Matthew 12:31-32 / Mark 3:28-30 Jesus said ‘And so I tell you, every kind of sin and slander can be forgiven, but blasphemy against the Spirit will not be forgiven. Anyone who speaks a word against the Son of Man will be forgiven, but anyone who speaks against the Holy Spirit will not be forgiven, either in this age or in the age to come.’ The Bible says being disrespectful, being rude, being rebellious to the Holy Spirit is ‘an unpardonable sin... an eternal sin... which requires eternal punishment...’

亵渎圣灵——意味着我们的心持续刚硬，抵挡那试图引导我们认罪悔改、信靠基督的圣灵。亵渎圣灵不仅仅是对圣灵说一些恶毒的话，而是持久地拒绝圣灵叫人知罪的工，而圣灵的工作正是揭露我们的罪并引导我们接受基督。
Blasphemy against the Spirit—means an ongoing hardening of our heart against the Holy Spirit who is trying to lead us to repent of sin and believe in Christ. Blaspheming the Holy Spirit is not simply saying something bad about the Holy Spirit, but is a persistent rejection of the convicting work of the Spirit, whose job is to expose our sin and lead us to accept Christ.

任何人如果对圣灵叫人知罪的职分硬着心，拒绝认罪悔改并在信心下转归基督，就犯下了永远无法被赦免的罪。那反复且不思悔改地拒绝圣灵为基督作见证的人，就是亵渎圣灵的人。一个人如果不上心悔改、不表达歉意、不认罪、不相信自己得罪了神，又怎能获得赦免呢？得罪神的人必须借着耶稣的救赎来寻求神的宽恕。
Anyone who hardens his or her heart against the convicting ministry of the Holy Spirit and those who refuse to repent their sin and turn to Christ in faith have committed a sin that never can be forgiven. The person who repeatedly and without repentance rejects the Holy Spirit’s testimony to Christ is the one who has blasphemed the Holy Spirit. How can someone be forgiven, if he or she doesn’t repent, doesn’t say sorry, doesn’t confess, doesn’t believe he or she who sins against God, must ask for God’s forgiveness through Jesus’ redemption.

简单的逻辑是：当我们拒绝圣灵时，我们就是拒绝基督，因此也就是在拒绝所有的赦免，所以这个罪就成了无法被赦免的罪。
The simple logic is: when we refuse the Holy Spirit, we refuse Christ, we are therefore refusing all forgiveness thus this sin is an unforgiven sin.
五旬节与大使命 / Pentecost and the Great Commission

在五旬节那天，圣灵戏剧性地降临，孕育了基督教会。“一阵大风吹过”和“火焰般的舌头”象征着神洁净且大有能力的同在。火焰代表了圣灵的炼净。
On the Day of Pentecost, the dramatic arrival of the Holy Spirit gave birth to the Christian church. The "mighty rushing wind" and the "tongues of fire" symbolize God's purifying and powerful presence. The fire represents the refinement of the Spirit.

在创世记第 11 章中，巴别塔的叛乱导致神变乱了人类的语言，从而将人类分散，不让他们聚集在一起继续犯罪。在五旬节那天，圣灵扭转了这一局面，使门徒能够说出那些“从天下各国来的虔诚犹太人”的本乡话。
In Genesis 11, the Babel Tower rebellion caused God to confuse human languages so to scatter human beings not to gather to keep on sinning. On the Day of Pentecost, the Holy Spirit reversed the situation to enable the disciples to speak the same languages of the "devout Jews from every nation".

众人用自己的母语听见宣扬“神的大作为”，因为圣灵使基督的福音能够被所有人理解，并与各种文化相契合。
The crowd heard the "mighty works of God" in their own mother tongues, as the Holy Spirit enables the Gospel of Christ to be accessible and culturally relevant to all peoples.

彼得引用先知约珥的话（约珥书 2:28-32）来解释这一事件，说明圣灵的降临开启了一个时代，其特点是在基督复临之前，救赎的扩展和神国的逐步建立。
Peter quotes the prophet Joel (Joel 2:28-32) to interpret the event, explaining that the coming of the Holy Spirit starts an era which is characterized by salvation and the progressive building of God's Kingdom before Christ's return.

神应许祂要将祂的灵浇灌凡有血气的，而不仅限于那些祭司、先知和君王，而是浇灌在儿女、少年人、老年人、少妇和老妇身上。神的灵将浇灌在每个人身上，因此每个人都将蒙受祝福，除非是那些拒绝并亵渎圣灵的人。
God Promised he would pour out his Spirit on all people, on all flesh, not just limiting on those priests and prophets and kings but on sons and daughters, boys and girls, young men and old men, young women and old women. The Spirit of God will be poured out on everyone, so everyone will be blessed, except for those who refuse and blasphemy the Holy Spirit.

“凡求告主名的，就必得救。”正是借着圣灵的恩赐，我们才能成为新造的人，成为属神的群体，成为以基督为中心的教会。我们是基督的身体，当且仅当神的灵——圣灵——在我们里面。
"everyone who calls upon the name of the Lord shall be saved," It is with the gift of the Holy Spirit that we will become a new creation, we will become a godly community, we will become a Christ centered Church. We are the body of Christ, because and only because the Spirit, the Holy Spirit is in us.

在约翰福音 16:7-15 中，耶稣解释说，直到基督完成祂的救赎工作，圣灵才会来。耶稣现在已经复活并升天了。祂舍命救赎的工作已经完成了。祂的门徒和信徒被赋予了大使命：“所以，你们要去，使万民作我的门徒……”若没有圣灵的帮助，没有人能够作门徒，也没有人能使人作门徒。圣灵现在降临来装备我们——你和我——去履行大使命。你准备好了吗？你被圣灵充满了吗？穿着红衣坐在这里，看到周围所有的火焰了吗？作为教会，作为基督徒，我们被点燃得有多旺盛？
In John 16:7–15, Jesus explains that the Holy Spirit cannot come until Christ completes His work of Salvation. Jesus is now risen and ascended. His work of sacrificial redemption was finished. His disciples and believers are given the Great Commission, ‘go and make disciples of all nations…’ no one can make and being made disciples without the help of the Holy Spirit. The Holy Spirit now comes to equip us, you and I… to fulfil the Great Commission. Are you ready? Are you filled with the Holy Spirit? Wearing red sitting here, seeing all the flames around? How fired up we are as a church, as a Christian?
## 在圣灵里合一 / Unity in the Holy Spirit

神希望世人认识祂的儿子，并获得耶稣付出如此高昂代价所带来的永恒祝福。神对每个人都是公平的，祂不偏待人。祂从每个支派、种族和语言中拣选犹太人和外邦人。圣灵将不同的人聚集在一起，圣灵在多样性中使群体联合。圣灵在合一中肯定多元性。
God wants the world to know His Son and to have the eternal blessings for which Jesus paid so dearly. God is fair to everyone and he plays no favoritism. He chooses Jews and Gentiles from every tribe, race, and language. The Holy Spirit brings different people together, the Holy Spirit unifies communities in variation. The Holy Spirit affirms diversity in unity.

朋友们，我们都是非常不同的。从七月开始，由 Kezha 牧师博士担任你们的新主任牧师（Vicar），这绝对是一件非常棒的事。但期间也会有许多需要调整适应的地方。你们将如何彼此理解并良好地同工，以推进耶稣的大使命呢？没有更好的办法，唯有祷告并顺从圣灵的指引。奉耶稣的名，阿们。
Friends, we are all very different. It will definitely be wonderful to have Dr Rev Kezha as your new vicar starting from July. But there will be a lot of adjustment also. How would you understand one another and work well with one another to advance Jesus’ Great Commission? There will be no better way, but to pray and follow the counsel of the Holy Spirit. In Jesus’ name, Amen.
