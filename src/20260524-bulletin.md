A Prayer on the Day of Pentecost
圣灵降临日祷告文

O God, who taught the hearts of your faithful people by sending to them the light of your Holy Spirit: grant us by the same Spirit to have a right judgement in all things, and evermore to rejoice in his holy comfort, through the merits of Christ Jesus our Saviour, who lives and reigns with you, in the unity of the same Spirit, one God, now and forever. Amen.

上帝啊，祢借着圣灵的光照教导祢忠心子民的心；求祢藉同一位圣灵赐我们在凡事上有正确的判断，并常常因祂神圣的安慰而喜乐；藉着我们的救主基督耶稣的功劳，祂与祢及同一位圣灵同享荣耀，永远作王，独一真神，直到永永远远。阿们。
