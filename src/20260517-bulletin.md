## A Prayer for the Church
## 为教会祷告

Our Heavenly Father, we lift Your church—Your bride—to You today and always. Thank You for the bond of love that unites us across differences. Fill us with Your Holy Spirit, deepening our love for You and for one another. Grant our leaders wisdom, integrity and strength to shepherd well. Give us courage and opportunities to share the Gospel, and may many turn to God and follow Jesus. For Christ’s sake. Amen.

亲爱的天父，我们今天并永远将祢的教会——祢的新妇——交托在祢手中。感谢祢赐下爱的联结，使我们虽有不同却仍合而为一。求祢以圣灵充满我们，加深我们对祢及彼此的爱。赐给教会领袖智慧、正直与力量，使他们能忠心牧养群羊。也求祢赐给我们机会与勇气传扬福音，使更多人归向神、跟随耶稣。奉基督的名祷告，阿们。
