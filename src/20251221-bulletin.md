## Let Us Pray

## 同心祷告

Almighty God, we pray that the course of this world may be so peaceably ordered through your guidance that your church may joyfully serve you in all godly quietness.
全能的上帝，我们祈求你引导世界的进程，使之充满和平，好让你的教会在敬虔与安宁中喜乐地事奉你。

May we, your people, who look forward to the Birthday of Christ, experience the joy of salvation and celebrate that feast with love and thanksgiving.
愿我们这些仰望基督降生的子民，经历救恩的喜乐，并以爱与感恩来庆祝这圣日。

We pray for your people in all nations and cultures; we shall weep and worry no more.
我们为各国各族的子民祷告，愿忧伤和惧怕不再捆绑我们。

The Lord will surely be gracious to us at the sound of our cry; He will cause His majestic voice to be heard.
主必因我们呼求的声音向我们施恩，使他威严的声音被听见。

We ask this through our Lord Jesus Christ, your Son, who lives and reigns with you and the Holy Spirit, one God, forever and ever. Amen.
我们这样祈求，是奉主耶稣基督的名；他与你和圣灵一同永生永王，独一的真神，直到永永远远。阿们。
