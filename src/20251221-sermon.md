# 以赛亚书 7:10–16（中英文对照，经文：简体和合本）

**10** 耶和华又晓谕亚哈斯说：  
**10** Again the Lord spoke to Ahaz, saying,

**11** 你向耶和华你的　神求一个兆头；或求显在深处，或求显在高处。  
**11** “Ask the Lord your God for a sign, whether in the deepest depths or in the highest heights.”

**12** 亚哈斯说：我不求；我不试探耶和华。  
**12** But Ahaz said, “I will not ask; I will not put the Lord to the test.”

**13** 以赛亚说：大卫家啊，你们当听！你们使人厌烦岂算小事，还要使我的　神厌烦吗？  
**13** Then Isaiah said, “Hear now, you house of David! Is it not enough to try the patience of humans? Will you try the patience of my God also?”

**14** 因此，主自己要给你们一个兆头：必有童女怀孕生子，给他起名叫以马内利。  
**14** Therefore the Lord himself will give you a sign: the virgin will conceive and give birth to a son, and will call him Immanuel.

**15** 到他晓得弃恶择善的时候，他必吃奶油与蜂蜜。  
**15** He will be eating curds and honey when he knows enough to reject the wrong and choose the right,

**16** 因为在这孩子还不晓得弃恶择善之先，你所憎恶的那二王之地必致见弃。  
**16** For before the boy knows enough to reject the wrong and choose the right, the land of the two kings you dread will be laid waste.

---

以马内利——以马内利，　神与我们同在；无论我们身处何地，无论在什么时间与季节，无论我们正在经历什么。  
Immanuel—Immanuel, God is with us wherever we go, whatever the time or season, and whatever we are going through.

无论是在高峰，还是在低谷；人生与事奉就像过山车，旋转翻腾，既带来欢乐，也时常伴随尖叫与惊吓。  
Whether we are in our highs or in our lows, life and ministry can be like a rollercoaster—spinning us around, giving us fun, but also screams and heart-stopping moments from time to time.

对《以赛亚书》第七章中“以马内利”的释经显示：这是在亚兰—以法莲战争期间赐给犹大王亚哈斯的预言。  
An exegesis of Isaiah 7 on Immanuel reveals a prophecy given to King Ahaz during the Syro-Ephraimite invasion.

这预言借着一个兆头应许　神的同在——“　神与我们同在”：有一位年轻女子将怀孕生子，名叫以马内利。  
It promises God’s presence (“God with us”) through a sign: a young woman bearing a son named Immanuel.

在这孩子分辨善恶之前，犹大将得以存留；后来，威胁犹大的诸国被亚述所灭。  
Before the boy knows good from evil, Judah would survive, fulfilled as Assyria conquered the threatening kingdoms.

这是一个近期的应验；而《马太福音》1:23 将其与耶稣的降生相连，显明“多重应验”。  
While a near-term fulfillment, Matthew 1:23 connects it to Jesus’ birth, presenting a “multiple fulfillment.”

基督是最终、完全的以马内利，彰显　神永恒地与祂百姓同在。  
Christ is the ultimate, perfect Immanuel, embodying God’s eternal presence with His people.

---

## 历史背景与即时应验（以赛亚书 7:1–17）  
## Historical Context & Immediate Fulfillment (Isaiah 7:1–17)

威胁：亚兰（叙利亚）与以色列（以法莲）结盟，企图废黜犹大王亚哈斯，另立傀儡王。  
**The Threat:** Syria (Aram) and Israel (Ephraim) formed an alliance to depose King Ahaz of Judah and install a puppet king.

　神的应许：以赛亚劝亚哈斯信靠　神，而非亚述，并应许入侵者必失败。  
**God’s Promise:** Isaiah tells Ahaz to trust God, not Assyria, promising the invaders would soon be defeated.

兆头：亚哈斯拒绝求兆头——也许是不在乎，也许是不相信；但　神仍赐下兆头。  
**The Sign:** Ahaz refuses to ask for a sign—perhaps he doesn’t care, perhaps he doesn’t believe—but God gives one anyway.

兆头的意义：在孩子能分辨善恶之前，亚兰和以色列之地必被撇弃，证明　神与亚哈斯同在并保护他的国。  
**The Sign’s Meaning:** Before the boy knows right from wrong, the lands of Syria and Israel will be deserted, confirming God’s presence and protection.

这在主前732年左右应验，亚述攻陷大马士革和以色列。  
This happened as Assyria conquered Damascus and Israel around 732 BCE.

---

## 弥赛亚式解释（多重应验）  
## Messianic Interpretation (Multiple Fulfillment)

马太福音：马太1:22–23明确将以赛亚7:14应用于耶稣的降生。  
**Matthew’s Gospel:** Matthew 1:22–23 explicitly links Isaiah 7:14 to Jesus’ birth.

终极的以马内利：耶稣的降生就是“　神与我们同在”的道成肉身。  
**Ultimate Immanuel:** Jesus’ birth embodies “God with us” in the Incarnation.

起初的以马内利是希望与同在的记号；基督则是完全、终极的彰显。  
The initial Immanuel served as a sign of hope and nearness; Christ is the perfect manifestation of that divine presence.

以赛亚书第七章呈现双重应验：即时的历史应验，以及在耶稣基督里的终极应验。  
Isaiah 7 presents a twofold fulfillment: an immediate historical sign and a greater future fulfillment in Jesus Christ.

耶稣是道，是创造主，也是救赎主；道成了肉身，住在我们中间。  
Jesus, the Word, makes God known—both Creator and Redeemer; the Word became flesh and dwelt among us.

---

所罗门王死后不久（约主前930年），以色列联合王国分裂为北国以色列与南国犹大。  
Almost two hundred years before Isaiah, after Solomon’s death (c. 930 BC), the united kingdom fractured into Israel and Judah.

两国之间的紧张与战争持续到以赛亚时代，并同时面对列强的威胁。  
Tensions and violence between the two kingdoms persisted, alongside threats from other nations.

亚述的侵略：二十岁的犹大王亚哈斯向亚述进贡并求助。  
**Assyrian Aggression:** Twenty-year-old Ahaz of Judah paid tribute to Assyria and asked for help.

列王纪下将亚哈斯描写为行恶之王，却拒绝加入反亚述联盟。  
2 Kings portrays Ahaz as wicked, yet he refused to join the alliance.

这场攻击称为亚兰—以法莲战争（约主前734年）。  
This attack is known as the Syro-Ephraimite War (c. 734 BC).

以赛亚的信息是信心与忍耐：敌人的计划不能成功。  
Isaiah’s message is one of faith and patience: the enemies’ plans will fail.

六十五年的应许对年轻的亚哈斯而言似乎太遥远。  
For a young king, a promise sixty-five years away seemed too distant.

因此　神赐下即时的兆头——以马内利。  
So God gave an immediate sign—Immanuel.

---

对亚哈斯而言，这预言关乎当下的危机；对我们而言，这是永恒的盼望。  
For Ahaz, the prophecy addressed an immediate crisis; for us, it offers eternal hope.

当　神与我们同在，困难仍是困难，但必按　神的时间与计划终止。  
When God is with us, troubles remain troubles—but they will end according to God’s time and plan.

马太福音宣告：耶稣出生就是“　神与我们同在”；复活后的主再次应许：“我就常与你们同在，直到世界的末了。”  
Matthew declares that Jesus is “God with us,” and the risen Lord reiterates: “I am with you always, to the end of the age.”

若　神与我们同在，若　神帮助我们，谁能敌挡我们呢？  
If God is with us, if God is for us, who can be against us?

以马内利——愿祂的平安、慈爱、喜乐与盼望与我们同在。  
Immanuel—may His peace, love, joy, and hope be with us.

因着基督，阿们。  
For Christ’s sake, Amen.
