## REFLECTION Matthew 13:24–30, 36–43 / 默想：马太福音 13:24–30, 36–43

In the Parable of the Weeds, Jesus reminds us that good and evil often grow side by side in this world. We may long for God to remove all that is wrong immediately, but God is patient, giving people time to repent and be transformed. Our calling is not to judge others, but to remain faithful, bearing the fruit of God's kingdom in our daily lives. Even when life seems unfair or confusing, we trust that God sees all things and, in his perfect justice, will one day set everything right. Until then, let us grow as faithful disciples, reflecting Christ's love, grace, and hope.
在“稗子的比喻”中，耶稣提醒我们，在这个世界上，善与恶常常并存。我们可能渴望神立即除掉所有错误的事物，但神是有耐心的，祂给人们时间去悔改和被改变。我们的呼召不是去审判他人，而是保持忠心，在日常生活中结出神国的果子。即使生活看似不公或令人困惑，我们坚信神鉴察一切，并且在祂完美的公义中，总有一天会拨乱反正。在那之前，让我们作为忠心的门徒不断成长，映照出基督的爱、恩典与盼望。

## PARISH PRAYER FOR GROWTH / 牧区增长祈祷文

God of mission, who alone brings growth to your church, send your Holy Spirit to give Vision to our planning, Wisdom to our actions, Joy to our worship, and power to our witness. Help our church to grow in numbers, in spiritual commitment to you, and in service to our local community.
使命的神啊，唯有祢让祢的教会增长，求祢差遣圣灵，赐予我们规划的异象、行动的智慧、崇拜的喜乐以及见证的能力。求祢帮助我们的教会在人数上增长，在对祢的属灵委身中成长，并更好地服务我们的本地社区。

## PRAYER FOR OUR CHILDREN / 为我们孩子的祈祷文

We hold in our hearts, the children and young people in our church, and broader community, made in your image and likeness, known to you and loved by you, whether our resources are plentiful or scarce, we commit to serving them more deeply. May our church become for them a safe space, to experience your life, your purpose and your love. May they encounter you in us. May we encounter you in them. And may it transform us both, Through Jesus Christ our Lord. Amen.
我们在心中记念我们教会及更广泛社区中的儿童和青少年。他们是按祢的形象和样式所造，为祢所知并被祢所爱。无论我们的资源是丰富还是匮乏，我们都承诺更深地服侍他们。愿我们的教会成为他们的安全避风港，去体验祢的生命、祢的旨意和祢的爱。愿他们在我们身上遇见祢，愿我们也在他们身上遇见祢。愿这种相遇让我们双方都得着改变。奉靠我们的主耶稣基督，阿们。
